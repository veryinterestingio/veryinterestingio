    Ellenorange A:

    ELI5: Most bug killers are poisons that people are able to neutralize, but most insects are not. Some of our pets (like dogs) can neutralize these poisons too, but others (like cats) can't, so we have to be careful when we use these poisons.

    Details: Permethrin is a commonly used pesticide. It's a neurotoxin, but most mammals are able to neutralize it in their liver, while most insects are not. Cats have a defect in glucuronosyltransferase, the enzyme that breaks permethrin down, so they are unable to break it down and are thus intolerant of permethrin.
