    We don't know.

    Recently, researchers have been understanding depression as the human body's natural response to chronic inflammation, and exercise has anti inflammatory properties. Other redditors have offered neurochemical related explanations which aren't necessarily wrong, but the simple answer is that much is still unknown regarding depression. We don't even know for sure what causes it or even the precise reasons why treatments work when they work.
