    Takancha A:

    Your hands and feet have a ton of nerves (especially close to the surface of your skin), mainly because it's just handy (no pun intended) to be able to feel what you're holding. Plus, if you grab something super hot or sharp, the "jerk" reflex has a higher chance of going off (keeping your hands or feet safer than for instance your elbow). This leads to being able to get super strong itches in your palms or soles, but this isn't entirely it. You also have a ton of skin there, especially your soles.

    Fun fact: The nerves in your palms and soles are actually why those areas of your body tend to be ticklish. Even your armpits have a ton of nerve endings.
