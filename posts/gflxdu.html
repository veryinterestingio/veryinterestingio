    Sehrja A:

    I once heard something that stuck with me about this very topic. Essentially it boiled down to the plan we make of “I’ll do it tomorrow” is less of a plan or idea and more of a mindset. When you wake up tomorrow your mindset is still the same: “I’ll do it tomorrow.” So the pattern continues. The only way to really break out of that mindset is change your mindset to “I’ll do it today.” Granted I’m not a psychologist, but this always made a lot of sense to me.
