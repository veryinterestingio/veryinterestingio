    eeetang A:

    Has to do with sleep/REM cycles. Basically you have like two modes of sleep that your body cycles through: a surface level sleep where you don’t really dream and can be woken up easier, a deeper REM sleep where you are dreaming and in a very deep slumber

    When you wake up at the end of a cycle during the lighter sleep phase, you more often feel well rested. The opposite is when you get woken up in the middle of REM sleep (this is usually do to bad alarms, inconsistent sleep schedule, etc.)
