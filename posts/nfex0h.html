    Black-Thirteen A:

    In addition to all the other great responses here, walking actually helps pump your blood.

    https://veinatlanta.com/your-second-heart/

    One of the disadvantages of evolving to stand upright is that your heart has to work so much harder to keep the same amount of blood circulating. That's why sitting or lying down feels restful, because your heart doesn't have to work so hard. But for when we are up and about, our calf muscles evolved to help pump blood down in the lowest extremities, farthest away from the heart, as we are walking around. Of course, you can't get that benefit if you're just standing there not pumping your muscles.
