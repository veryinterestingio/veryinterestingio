    NEW
    Q:

    My whole life i have obviously thought it was normal that house hold pets like to be petted/scratched. But every now in then on reddit, i see animal such as birds, snakes, gekkos, and even a platypus being petted by humans. Do all/most animal enjoys it? Or are they just letting it happen out of fear?

    funwithallama A:

    On the one hand, it’s a bonding experience. In most mammals, the mum grooms her babies, and you grooming your pet is similar to that experience.

    On the other hand, you can scratch places they can’t and that is precious. Ever try to massage the outside of your dogs ear? Most of them go absolutely nuts. Scratch a horse on the back or withers and they go crazy as well.

    Edit since several people asked: the withers is the bony part on a horses back between the shoulder blades, at the base of the mane
