    pistonrings A:

    Ethanol is a diuretic. That means it makes you pee a lot. When you pee, you lose sodium, which leads to a condition hyponatremia, which is medical for lack of sodium. Salty foods help to replace the lost sodium.

    BurnOutBrighter6 A:

    In addition to these answers, being drunk also numbs your sense of smell/taste (it IS an anesthetic after all!). So things taste more bland than usual. To make up for this, you crave strongly-flavoured foods, which salty/fried foods often are. That's why a lot of "bar food" is flavourful stuff like tacos and hot wings.
