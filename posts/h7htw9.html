  • eli5 What is the difference between a proverb, aphorism, epigram, and adage?

    NEW
    Q:

    Math major here haha. Are they not the same thing?

    saxy-french-horn A:

    A proverb is typically a memorable, commonplace piece of advice: "Never wake a sleeping bear."

    An aphorism is more an observation: "A bear in spring is always hungry."

    An epigram is made to be clever or funny: "Always respect mother nature. Especially when she weighs 400 pounds and is guarding her baby."

    An adage is similar to a proverb, but tends to be less poetic and more of a definition: "The best way to be kind to bears is not to be very close to them."
