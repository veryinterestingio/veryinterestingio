  • why do truckers put a twist in the ratchet strap when they strap something down?

    NEW
    Q:

    I've just noticed this recently. It seems intentional as sometimes with really large items all the straps are twisted the same way. Does it have something to do with air? Like does this prevent the strap from flapping in the wind or something?

    Old_timey_brain A:

    The twist helps with air flow.

    A flat strap will catch the air and vibrate badly.

    The twist allows a path for the air to follow more smoothly.
