    Q:

    ELI5: Why does most household garbage have the same distinct odor even though everyone’s garbage consists of different things?

    TokyoJokeyo A:

    Different kinds of plants or meats will decompose pretty similarly, and what you're smelling is the beginning of that process. So even though everyone's is a little different the end result is not so varied. But you definitely have variations in how household garbage smells, too; e.g. diapers are certainly distinct.
