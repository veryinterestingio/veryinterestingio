    Q:

    ELI5: What’s happening to fat cells as a body uses them during weightloss?

    Wormsblink A:

    They shrink in size. You still have (around) same number of fat cells as before, they’re just smaller.

    That’s why gaining weight after weight loss is not uncommon. The fat cells can just store more fat & grow in size again.

    Edit: this link explains it pretty well https://well.blogs.nytimes.com/2017/02/17/are-fat-cells-forever/
