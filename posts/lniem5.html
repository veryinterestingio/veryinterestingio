    iamspartaaaa A:

    ELI5: What causes this? Brain. You can think of it as responsive sympathetic reflexes. Why it is your stomach? 1- Because blood is being taken away from your stomach to you skeletal muscles. 2- Vagus nerve. Why it happens so instantly? Again, Vagus nerve.

    Not ELI5: Since Vagus innervates GIT, Heart, Bronchial tree, Intestines and ascending and transverse 2/3rd of large intestine, stimulated Vagus can cause pretty much all of it instantly. Other conditions that might occur to compliment the “drop feeling” may include: Tachycardia (Increased heart rate), Tachypnea (Higher breathing rate), Xerostomia (Lack of salivation) and Bronchodilation (Dilation of bronchus, causes Increased air flow to the lungs).
