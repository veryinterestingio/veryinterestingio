  • Medicine What methods are used to trace the origin of a new virus?

    NEW
    Q:

    What sort of methods of investigation/experimentation are used to determine/trace the origin of a newly discovered virus? That is to say, When a new virus is discovered, how do we find out where it first appeared?

    CleanPostingHistory A:

    The origin is estimated by comparing genomes of the virus collected at different times and places. By applying mathematical models of how the viral genome drifts and adapts, it's possible to work backwards and choose the most likely ancestor.

    See Genomic epidemiology of novel coronavirus (nCoV) on Nextstrain for an example of how this information is collated and presented.

    https://nextstrain.org/ncov
