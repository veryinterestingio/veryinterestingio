    A smile doesn't just mean happiness. It's a social queue that can show many things including being nervous.

    Also, other apes use smiling in a similar way, such as chimps.

    So, like many human traits, they were adopted from previous ancestral behaviors (like "smiling" to show your teeth as a sign of aggression) to become much more social.
