    Every year congress must pass and the President must approve a budget which defines how much money the government will spend. If this doesn’t happen only the very most essential functions continue and everything else stops because the government isn’t allowed to spend money.

    In this case leaders in congress had a deal to pass a budget that would fund the government into February but Trump convinces republicans in the Senate to push for his pet project, the Border Wall.

    So what does this accomplish? In this case Trump and some Republicans are betting that the shutdown will cause congress to give in to his demands.
