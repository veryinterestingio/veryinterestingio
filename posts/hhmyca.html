    Q:

    Eli5 :Why does sleeping with a pillow or blanket between your legs at night feel so comfortable and relaxing?

    fiendishrabbit A:

    If you're a side-sleeper then putting a pillow/blanket between your knees will prevent your hips from tilting which in turn will help keep your spine in a neutral position. This will help all the muscles attached to your spine (especially those in the lower back) relax.
