    ELI5: How do weighted blankets help people fall asleep/sleep solidly through the night?

    pixielatedheart A:

    The research hasn't been done very deeply yet, to be honest. The studies that support it are small, and it's not the kind of experiment that a good control group can be set up.

    That said, here is the theory :

    "Deep touch pressure" has been studied as a aid for Autism spectrum kids. Basically light stimulation (tickling loose clothes, the wind, etc.) excites your nervous system. If you are already anxious, it increases your fight/flight response. Deep pressure (hugging, a tight compression shirt, or a weighted blanket) pushes past that triggering response and actually sooths your nervous system, encouraging the release of calming neurotransmitters.
