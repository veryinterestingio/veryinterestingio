    Kobe3rdAllTime A:

    When you play blackjack you're playing against the house.

    When you play poker at a casino you're playing against other people. The casino makes money by taking a percentage of each pot. They don't actually care who wins each hand because they're not involved.

    DarkAlman A:

    Card counting is one of the few legal methods to beat the house. So long as you are only doing the math in your head and not using a calculator, computer or anything to track the count.

    Casinos by law can kick out anyone they want. If they suspect you of card counting they'll kick you out "for not playing fair", basically they just don't want to lose the money.

    With Poker on the other hand the house doesn't care as much because only 1 person at the table is leaving with the cash. The house makes money regardless of who wins.
