    Hyperspacehost A:

    Sleeping in one giant lump is a fairly modern phenomenon, possibly due to electric and gas lighting making it cheap and easy to carry your daytime well into the evening. There’s a lot of evidence that people used to go to sleep in the evening, get up at midnight for some candle-lit reading or a shag, then go back to sleep until the early morning. It’s probably the natural way of doing things, but we have this new social structure of going to bed later and sleeping through.
