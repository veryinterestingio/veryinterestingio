    chainsaw_monkey A:

    A lot of the experimentally derived smallest genomes lose function. Meaning they can only grow in very specific conditions. A bacterial genome has a lot of metabolic variety so it can turn on genes depending on what food source is available and what the environment is like. As you remove genes, you also remove survival options.

    Compare to a car, what is the minimal car? Do you need doors, windshield, catalytic converter, air bags. You can strip it down but eventually you get a vehicle that only works in a very limited manner.
