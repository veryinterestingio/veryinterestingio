    Q:

    ELI5: How do Hawaii and other remote islands deal with trash?

    TournantDangereux A:

    The same way municipalities everywhere deal with their trash:

    1. Bury most of it. Landfills are used on islands, just like anywhere else.

    2. Burn some of it. Some islands build trash-to-power incinerators.

    3. Ship some of it to another place. For HAZMAT and valuable reclaimable materials, it makes economic sense to send these materials to a remote processing location.
