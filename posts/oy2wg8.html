    ELI5: How do Pop Rocks pop in your mouth?

    ThunderDrop A:

    Fun fact, pop rocks were created as an attempt at instant soda.

    The idea was you pour them into water. The dissolving candy would sweeten it and the released CO2 would make it fizzy.

    It didnt work, but turned into a pretty fun candy.

    MoFauxTofu A:

    They make the candy in a tank of high pressure gas.

    Imagine if you were in a spaceship and you put a lid on an empty jar. The pressure inside the jar would be the same as inside the spaceship.

    Now if you went out into space and took the lid off the jar, air would rush out.

    It's the same idea with poprocks. They make them under high pressure and small pockets of high pressure gas gets trapped in the candy. When the candy cracks / melts, the gas escapes and makes the popping noise.

    The little voids in them are filled with gas under pressure. When you get them wet, the sugar weakens and the pressure causes a fracture.
