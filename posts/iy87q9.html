    Farnsworthson A:

    Where these aren't simply obvious things for a bored person to do (which I'm sure many of them are), what you're describing is "cultural" behaviour. Basically, we have an innate tendency to imitate, to some degree, at least, the behaviour of those around us whom we perceive as in some way part of "our" group. It doesn't have to be useful behaviour.
