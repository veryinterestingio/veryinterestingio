    MooseBayou A:

    Cut a 45^^0 V in the middle of the fingernail. Right at the apex of radius of the nail. Right in the middle so you leave a small gap at the end of the nail.

    This will cause the nail to desire a closure of that V. It will take time, though. 3-4 weeks.

    • I know this sounds funny. I get that. I am 59. I had huge troubles with big-toe hangnails when I was around 15 from athletic pursuits. A neighbor Dad taught me this. I have always had a small V on my big-toe nails, and I have not had a recurrence of the dreaded ingrown nail. Not in almost 45 years.

    • For immediate relief, try taking a sterilized safety pin and tucking little pieces of Q-Tip ends into the affected corner of the nail, until it "jacks" the nail upward, away from the skin. This will also help to push the inflammation out and away from the cuticle, and you can get a clipper on that edge that's digging in. Pour 91% Isopropyl all over it. Ice it. Cut that snaggle-fucking edge of the nail as soon as you can get a clipper edge underneath it, and you will feel immediate relief. Keep that sucker clean. Ice again to deter more swelling and bleeding, if shit comes to grunt.

    • Good luck.
