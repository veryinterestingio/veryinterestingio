    ELI5: What is the scientific reasoning behind getting "hangry"?

    bguy74 A:

    When you are hungry your body releases a whole bunch of hormones to get your body to metabolize stuff in to glucose, to feed your cells. Two of these (epinephrine and cortisol) hormones also happen to be our "stress hormones". So...when hungry you're likely act like you do when "stressed", which...is...irritable!
