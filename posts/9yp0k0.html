    Q:

    Do snakes preferentially bend to the left or to the right when forming loops?

    zfddr A:

    A really interesting question. I found a study from 2003 that looked at this called ‘Handedness’ in snakes? Lateralization of coiling behaviour in a cottonmouth, Agkistrodon piscivorus leucostoma, population

    I'm not sure it if it is open access, but the main result that only 3 out of 30 snakes displayed any significant "Handedness" in this population. There does seem to be some bias in the snakes, but the results are not statistically significant.
