    Ipissedonjesus A:

    Hunger is not linear. That is, if you don't eat you don't simply feel hungrier and hungrier until you die of starvation.

    It's related to hormonal shifts, and if you have ever fasted for a long period of time you realize that you only experience hunger in a few hourly episodes at certain points in the day, and the hunger goes away for periods of time at other points in the day.

    So, if you are on an IV providing nutrients, you will not be hungry all the time, but you will be hungry sometimes
