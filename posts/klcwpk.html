    Himantolophus A:

    Pompeii was pretty humongous - it was a city and the archeological site covers about 66 hectares (163 acres) of which 49 hectares have already been excavated. So it's pretty huge and there's lots left to be discovered. Nearby Herculaneum has only had about 20% excavated due to being covered by a pyroclastic flow which buried the city under about 25 m (82 feet) mud, compared with 'only' 11m (36 feet) ash and pumice that covered Pompeii.
