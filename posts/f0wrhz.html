    ELI5: Why do some nights of sleep seem to pass in the blink of an eye, yet other nights you’ll wake up at 6, go back to sleep till 7, and it feels like several more hours have passed?

    MikuEmpowered A:

    sleep have 4 stages. At stage 3, it's called Deep Stage, meaning you basically black out, and have 0 idea on whats going on. Extremely difficult to wake someone up in this stage, a Good night sleep will spend a good period of time going between stage 2 and 3, thus it feels extremely short because you have no awareness.

    But sometimes when you sleep, whether stress or anxiety, you don't spend much time in deep state, you arn't fully shut off, thus you have some awareness of time passage, thus the sleep feels extremely long.
