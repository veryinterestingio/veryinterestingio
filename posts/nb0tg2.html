    Nira_Meru A:

    To be clear: it’s not. The back of the head directly above the vertebrae is a far better place to strike if needing to knock out an opponent. It is generally more difficult to get to but it is also banned in MMA because not only can blows to the back of the head knock you out they also can cause traumatic brain injuries at a much higher rate than other forms of punching.

    In particular the most damaging punch would be what is called a hammer fist, directly to this portion of the skull, because of the leverage able to be generated and the relative weakness at absorbing concussive force this part of the head has.
