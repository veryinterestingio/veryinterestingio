    Q:

    Have wondered this my whole life.

    Applejuiceinthehall A:

    U and V used to be used as the same letter. Vs were at the beginning of the word and Us were in the word. So a word like Upon was written Vpon and have was haue. The W used to be written VV or UU and worked the same way as the v and u.

    Don't even get me started on Y.
