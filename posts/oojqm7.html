    phigene A:

    It has everything to do with the concentration and absorption. When you smoke (anything), most of the drug does not absorb into your body, and is exhaled. Which is why you can get high on second hand smoke. When you eat a properly prepared edible (one that is prepared a low temp and uses oil or other agents that bond to THC) the amount of THC that make it into your system can be significantly higher.

    Also, there is no immediate effect. When you smoke, you get a near instant buzz, which allows you to moderate your intake better. You know when you've had enough, and can stop there. When you eat an edible, you don't know how high you're going to get until it's too late to do anything about it.
