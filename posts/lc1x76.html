  • ELi5: What causes someone to feel great after a little sleep or still tired after a lot of sleep sometimes?

    NEW
    Q:

    You know when you sleep for a few hours and get up to pee and feel like you could wake up then and there and sometimes you go to sleep and sleep for 10 to 12 hours and feel dead

    Red_Beard_Rising A:

    There can be a few reasons, but the simple ELI5 reason would be...

    The sleep cycle runs in about 90 minute increments from light sleep, to deep sleep, and back again. If you wake up during a light sleep phase, you'll feel more rested. It's when your body naturally wants to wake up. If you wake up from deep sleep, your body doesn't want to wake up, so you still feel tired.
