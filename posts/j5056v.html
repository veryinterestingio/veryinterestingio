    Goldenoir A:

    Lack of sleep = Pale skin and fluid buildup. Blood circulation is a lot less efficient. Skin under eyes is very thin. Basically fluids buildup in blood vessels, so they enlarge under very thin and pale skin which makes your skin surrounding your eyes appear darker.

    How to remedy it? Sleep 8 hours, no less, no more. Oversleeping can cause the same effects. Hydrate, hydrate, and hydrate more.
