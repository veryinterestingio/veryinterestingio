    Onehandedclapper A:

    Egg shells have pores so oxygen can pass through. There is a mechanism in which the more the organism develops - the more the organism needs oxygen - and the egg pores become larger or more numerous to allow more oxygen to pass. This causes the egg shell to become weaker as the organism becomes stronger and the organism eventually breaks free and breathes oxygen by itself.
