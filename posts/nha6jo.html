    It's not really accurate to say people are "carriers" of COVID, implying some kind of long term equilibrium state. The human immune system will always fight covid and the battle will be won or lost.

    The spectrum of symptoms will be determined by a lot of factors. Some may have had previous exposure to a similar virus. Some people will just not recognize the symptoms on top of their normal bronchitis, etc.
