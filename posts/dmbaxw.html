    Barnaclebills A:

    Orthodontic technician here! The glue is removed either with a specialized tool called “an adhesive removing plier” that flakes the glue off, which takes while, or a dentist drill that has a special bur that pretty much sands it off to the enamel (without being the kind of bur that has the purpose of removing enamel like some other burs have). Then a polishing bur gets put on to smooth out any remaining roughness from the first bur.
