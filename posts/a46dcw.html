    PatrickRsGhost A:

    Like others mentioned, constant exposure. But it's not just to one particular beer. Over time you've probably tried different brands, different flavors/types, and even how they were poured or served (can, bottle, or pulled from a draft).

    The same can be said for any type of food. Take for example broccoli. I know a lot of people, both kids and adults, who hate broccoli. They've probably only had it one or two ways, but when you try it served other ways, either as a side dish or as an ingredient in an entree, you might come to like broccoli.
