    Tidus810 A:

    There is a phenomenon called “pica” whereby someone who is considerably iron deficient will crave very unusual things, classically ice chips. Infants and young children have also been reported to eat dirt. So there’s that.

    https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/m/pubmed/11001006/

    Also semi-related are folks with diabetes insipidus, a disease involving either lack of ADH (anti-diuretic hormone) or renal resistance to ADH. The result is an inability to concentrate ones urine and subsequent polyuria (excessive urination). These patients will crave ice cold water to preserve normal blood levels of sodium and overall osmolality.

    https://www.endocrinologyadvisor.com/endocrinology-metabolism/diabetes-insipidus/article/596107/
