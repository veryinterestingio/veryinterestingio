    I_Am_Noot A:

    I work in a large warehouse retail type store. Over 70% of our revenue is from Business2Business sales, rather than Business2Customer. This is the main driver of being “open” between 7am and 5pm on weekdays. Many of these businesses will have an official “purchasing officer” who comes into the store to purchase the items they need. If we weren’t open they’d simply go to the next store that is.
