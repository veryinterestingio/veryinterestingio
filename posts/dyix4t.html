    Q:

    ELI5: Why do sneezes "build up" as opposed to being instant/near-instant like most other reflexes?

    Ipride362 A:

    Because Sneezing is not a reflex. It is a muscle spasm. And for the same reasons an orgasm takes a minute or two, a sneeze has to build up as the muscle spasm widens and releases.
