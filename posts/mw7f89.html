    Q:

    Is there any progress in tinnitus treatment? I have seen some 10 year old posts here talking about possible cures. The post was archived so I couldn't write on it. I'd like to know what has changed so far in 10 years.

    helloreddits456464 A:
    1. OTO-313 currently in Phase 2
    2. Hearing restoration - FX-322 in phase 2 though failed for multiple injections, but still works for single injections.
    3. Bimodal neuromodulation. Mixed Results
    4. KCNQ2/3 channel openners. RL-81 from University of Pittsburgh or XEN1101 from Xenon pharmaceuticals.
    5. Deep Brain stimulation/Neuralink mentions tinnitus
