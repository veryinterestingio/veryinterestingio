      You also have to consider different manufacturers who will use different key shapes - for example, different grooves on the sides, which means only that manufacturer's key will fit. Even if two keys have the same "code", it doesn't matter if the grooves are not the same.

      There are also entirely different styles of locks, like disk detainer cores, dimple lock cores, tubular lock cores, and locks with other unusually shaped keys. Manufacturers may use cores with more or fewer pins, disks, etc. so there might be 5 pins or 12 pins. They might have more or fewer positions those pins can be in.
