    • CrazyLikeGollum

      Benzodiazepines have a very similar mechanism of action in that they promote the activity of GABA. Benzos are probably a little more potent in that regard.

      Xanax, Valium, and Diazepam are examples of benzos. If anxiety is something you struggle with, it may be worth talking to your doctor about one of those.

      Note: Benzos are highly addictive, have some nasty withdrawal effects, and can interact poorly with other medications. Definitely talk to a doctor before taking one of these drugs.
