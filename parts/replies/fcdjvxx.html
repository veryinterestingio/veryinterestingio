    Found the German. For everyone scratching their head: that's Scrooge McDuck. In the German localizations he is called "Dagobert Duck", probably because A Christmas Carol us not very well known here, so the reference would not have made a lot of sense, and comic readers would not have known how to pronounce "Scrooge" correctly (German speakers might read it as "Skroh-geh")
