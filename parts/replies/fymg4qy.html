      Acclimatization is real. I used to live next to a bus stop in a major city. Every five minutes a bus would stop. Less than two weeks after moving in, I only really noticed when a bus with extremely squeally brakes would stop. It was actually disturbing after midnight when the busses stopped. Too quiet.

      Rain and steady wind have consistent "white noise" in them that the human brain finds soothing. Construction and other noises occur at random times that the brain finds jarring and doesnt like. A gentle rain is much more soothing than a hurricane or driving rain.
