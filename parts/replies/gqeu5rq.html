      No, it doesn't have to take that long, but there's little motivation to improve it.

      Cable would be the easiest to make faster, by basically putting a cable modem in there so you can ask for a chunk of video and immediately get a keyframe instead of just waiting for the next keyframe.

      For something like satellite TV, the receiver would have to be MUCH more expensive, as you'd have to make a new receiver that listens to all the channels simultaneously, and buffers everything since the last keyframe.
