    I'm right handed, and have dysgraphia (dyslexia, but for handwriting/drawing). I only know which hand I write with because I have a callus where the pen sits when I write. There's muscle memory, but if I have to actually think about it, I get confused.

    But am EXCELLECT at navigation. I can visualize where we are going or what we need to do to get there. Usually don't need a map if I've been there before.

    I always say the wrong word for left and right.

    When I'm telling someone directions, I will tell them with full confidence to take a left up ahead, while picturing in my head that we are taking a right. I often don't notice my verbal error until we miss the turn or they start switching lanes the wrong directions.
