    This is the answer. Lots of discussion about evolution and favorable phenotypes, but that's not the question here. The arrangement of the face is not a trait which is selected for, it is a direct result of embryonic developmental processes.

    People who talk about the function of each organ and what it "needs" are just describing their own personal assumptions. Those are psuedo-scientific answers that are not grounded in an understanding of the underlying biological mechanisms at a cellular level.
