    Carbon doesn't have the right electronic configuration, and so dirt, wood etc. can't be magnetized by applying an external field.

    Pretty much every earth material contains some kind of ferromagnetic mineral, e.g. magnetite, hematite, goethite, and pyrrhotite. So dirt probably carries a remanent magnetization, although its probably pretty weak.
