    • REDDIT-ROCKY

      Yes. This is a slurry seal. So in your case it sounds like rather than use a premium asphalt mix with high grade materials, they’ve used a cheaper alternative, possibly recycled, then added a final layer - the slurry seal.

      This final coating is used to seal the pavement from water ingress and provide the anti skid properties that the layer below cannot.

      It’s typically a very thin layer than should be good for up to a decade depending on traffic loading.
