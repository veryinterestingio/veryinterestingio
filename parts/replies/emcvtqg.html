    Not just that but frequent password changes drastically increase the likelihood that they will be stolen, because people forget them and write them down.

    A password you keep in your head for 8 years is way more secure than one you write down every 2 months. Especially because if someone does steal your password, changing every 2 months is nowhere near enough, you can insinuate yourself permanently in that network with, like, an hour of access.
