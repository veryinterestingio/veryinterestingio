    • catwhowalksbyhimself

      Because the large intestine does absorb the food. It's job is to deal with the waste. The small intestine absorbs nutrients and such into the bloodstream and then the large intestine squeezes the leftovers, absorbs most of the water and expells the resulting poo out of your body.

      To put it in another way, your small intestine only works with a wet paste, but your large intestine dries everything up. They are in that order because they only work in that order.
