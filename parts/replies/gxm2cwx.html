      It certainly can be a scary thing. I would make sure to not approach the subject in a ‘worse case scenario’ mindset and try not to make any assumptions because it can be very harmful to them. That person was a friend but they never really spoke to me again after that because they were so bothered by the idea of hallucinating and the negative stigma around it that. They couldn’t get past their preconceived notions of what it was like to consider my viewpoint and how for me it’s just normal.
