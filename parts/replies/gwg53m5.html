    many people fail to understand structural unemployment too...behind getting aligned there is also the change in job processes...the most literal example being handmade textiles to machine made textiles...the machines didn't "destroy" the handmade industry but they did cause many of the people who did it by hand and maybe weren't that good at it to have to move to another profession or learn to use the machine to make the textiles....in essence the workers job and job location changed but the job itself was still making textiles
