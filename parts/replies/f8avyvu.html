      There were actually very good reasons for all of this. It may not be immediately obvious to an outsider but the standards body works to accommodate the needs of its members.

      DCP lets you re use components across different compositions. This is a big deal when it comes to versioning which is a huge deal for big studios that have their films in many countries.

      The same general structure and concepts were adapted to being usable upstream in the workflow and this has been standardized as IMF.
