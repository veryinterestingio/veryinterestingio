    To expand on this - because they don't dissolve in water, permanent markers generally use acetone as a solvent instead - it dissolves the ink, but evaporates quickly when exposed to air. If you need to clean permanent marker off a smooth surface, you can clean it off with acetone (often sold as nail polish remover), or in a pinch, draw over it with another marker, and then wipe it off quickly before the ink from that one dries too.
