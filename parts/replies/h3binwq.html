    Definitely. There's a lot of other posters talking about the body going into a resting phase, and I don't think that's what anybody experiences. When driving not only do you have to be alert at all times, but you're also constantly using making very small movements with your muscles to adjust the steering wheel, pedals, even just keeping your posture up while seated is expending energy.

    In athletic training there's always the comparison of doing a really difficult thing a few times versus doing a really easy thing a few dozen times. Driving is like doing an infinitely simple thing a few million times an hour. It's absolutely expending energy in ways that are unlike most everything else we do.
