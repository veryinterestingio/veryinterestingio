    Bladder rupture can also occur. This is seen occasionally in car accidents when someone has a very full bladder, say after drinking at a bar excessively. Rupture can either be into the abdominal cavity and require surgery to fix, or can be into the extraperitoneal space and might heal with bladder decompression with a foley catheter for a time.
