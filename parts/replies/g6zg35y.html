    Mass does not increase, this is a common misconception. In SR mass is invariant, being the Lorentz norm of the 4-momentum, all inertial frames measure the same mass for a given particle.

    The origin of this misconception is (I assume) an attempt at making classical sense of the form of the spatial components of relativistic momentum.

    That is to make it look more familiar to the classical p=mv
