    Kinda a rule of thumb if you make it twice as strong it'll last ten times longer.

    One consideration is the ride into space is a harsh one. High G forces and lots of heavy vibration. After that not so much.

    Three other considerations are power, altitude control, and communications. Those are must not fail systems and they get a bigger part of the weight budget than everything else. As long as they are still going the thing is technically still alive even if other systems have failed.
