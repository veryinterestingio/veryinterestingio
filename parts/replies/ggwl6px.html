        And even that isn’t a guarantee, as there are cases which present after 10 days of isolation. Initial studies showed that 97.5% of cases presented within 11.5 days. That means more than 1 in 50 cases present with symptoms after 11.5 days since exposure. In rare cases, some people become symptomatic even after 14 days since exposure.
