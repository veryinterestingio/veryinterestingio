    This. Worked in games for 15 years. ELI5 version is characters are made up of points in 3D space in the “box” of the game, and the rendering engine loses the start and end points of where to draw or connect shapes. Also related to why you see characters in T pose in strange loading situations. T pose is generally the “starting point” where the animation engine knows where everything is on the character before assigning physics and animation commands
