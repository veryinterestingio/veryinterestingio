  • kindanormle

    Yes, they can be transplanted more than once, the "gotcha" is that each transplantation reduces the likelyhood of success due to damage to the organ from surgery and stress. If given a choice between a "fresh" organ with the highest chance of success and another organ whose chances are lower, doctors will choose the one with the highest chance of success.
