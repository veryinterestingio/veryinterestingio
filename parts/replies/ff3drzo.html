    Small scale traditional hydro may only divert a portion of the river/creek through piping, allowing the majority of the flow to remain as is.

    A turbine is installed at the bottom of pipe and the effluent water reconnects with the original creek/river.

    This is common for river/creeks with significant natural elevation changes, where the hydrostatic pressure is the main energy driver instead of volumetric flowrate.
