    Just wanting to correct a bit of a movie myth here. The force isn’t hundreds of times stronger, it is a pressure difference of 1 atmosphere.

    The difference between 2 atmospheres of pressure and 1 (from 10.3metres underwater compared to the surface), is the same difference in force you get between 1 atmosphere at sea level and the ~0 of space.

    A gentle sucking* force, but still not one our bodies are meant to survive for very long in as you mentioned above with the boiling at room temperature.

    *Edit: sucking force is misleading, we’re just not being pushed together anymore.
