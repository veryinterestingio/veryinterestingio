    In some ways this will self filter, as those who are putting themselves into risky situations might feel that they should go out of their way to get the vaccine, meaning they will have an outsized reduction on the transmission rate.

    I think that a large portion of this will be off-set by the cohort who is purposely acting in risky ways. For example, the person who refuses to wear a mask for political reasons may also believe there is no need for them to get a vaccine as well. This subset will continue to be likely super-spreaders only mitigated to the extent that people who are exposed to them have immunity.
