    • RhiR2020

      We had a disaster when our neighbours discovered our builder’s apprentice had put the measuring tape on the wrong site peg during set out. As a result, our wooden frames and top storey board sat out in the weather for 6 months while we organised what to do. The board was a little mouldy by then, so we clove oiled the whole lot and all the mould died. Everything went ahead after that, as we purchased and subdivided a small bit of the neighbour’s land and kept going rather than ripping it all down. We were very lucky.
