    By extended i believe he means ultrawide, displays with 21:9 aspect ratio and above.

    To answer the question its all about the cables bandwidth being higher. Hdmi 2.0 only supports 4k at 60hz, while display port 1.4 supports 4k at 120hz and 8k at 60hz.

    Many higher resolution monitors will allow you to connect with hdmi, but as a result they will not be able to meet both their max refresh and resolution at the same time.

    A 1440p 21:9 ultrawide is a 5Mp (megapixel) display, close enough to 4Ks 8Mp that it benefits from the extra bandwidth of display port.
