      so hawking radiation relies on the concept of "quantum foam"

      basically, quantum variation can sometimes generate pairs of charged particles where they shouldnt be, the universe will take some energy in a vague region and say "this energy is actually two particles with opposing charges".

      these particles sometimes appear at the very border of escaping the black hole, and rather than recombining into nothing, one escapes and the other one falls in, allowing some mass to flee the black hole.

      with large black holes, this is extremely slow, because the distances involved make these perfect pairs really unlikely.
