    • CountingMyDick

      Depends on the application. The really high rates of fire are mostly used on high-speed aircraft that might only be over the target for a fraction of a second, or only on-target against another high-speed aircraft for even shorter amount of time. They would only fire in really short bursts, and need to get a lot of rounds out in that time to be effective.

      It mostly doesn't make sense to fire that fast from slow-moving land vehicles. Too hard to keep on target, burns through ammo too fast, etc.
