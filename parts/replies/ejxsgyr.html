    Hello! Your English is very good, except that you keep using “it’s”. “it’s” with an apostrophe always means “it is.” So, if the sentence doesn’t make sense with “it is”, don’t put the apostrophe. To make “it” possessive, you just add the s; no apostrophe.

    Autocorrect always adds an apostrophe, which is very frustrating, so maybe it was just that :).
