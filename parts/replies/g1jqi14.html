    • 9T3

      There’s kind of two questions here, but in general someone that’s physically fit/healthy is less likely to experience complications from taking MDMA, or most drugs for that matter (excluding allergies etc.).

      In terms of resting heart rate being lower in the case of someone being fit, the answer is kind of yes but that’s also true regardless of what drugs you’re on. Measuring your resting heart rate is a good way to benchmark progress in your individual fitness journey but it’s not necessarily the best measure of fitness compared to others. V02 max is considered a better measure of cardiovascular fitness by many, but it’s not worth getting into.
