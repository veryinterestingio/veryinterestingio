      Some say the "benefit" is less hand-off, which the industry claims is where mistakes/miscommunication arises. It's certainly possible to have one person do night blocks for a week (12 hrs shifts) and one person does days, then swap. There are more hand-offs that way.

      Unfortunately, we don't get to rest for a couple of days. We get a post-call day (a "day off" after working 24 hrs, where we mostly just sleep), but we're expected to be back to work the following day. There isn't enough staff to have several days off. We're lucky to get a full weekend off (we call them "golden weekends").
