        Welsh and Latin are both Indo-European languages but really are only related insofar as having proto-Indo-European as a common ancestor.

        Welsh does have a lot of words of ecclesiastical Latin origin and also English words of Latin origin that were both absorbed into the language later. So there can be some Latin “false friends” in Welsh.
