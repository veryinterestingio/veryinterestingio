  • Vanniv_iv

    Additionally, horns like trumpets didn't used to have valve buttons.

    Instead, you'd own a set of different pieces of tubing to insert into the trumpet at different times to change its key.

    This is why many older pieces would have a section in, say, Trumpet in Bb, and then like a key change would just switch to, say, Trumpet in Eb.

    You'd quickly pull out the tuning bit, stick in a different-length one, and continue playing in the new transposition.

    Of course, this is an awful way to notate music today.
