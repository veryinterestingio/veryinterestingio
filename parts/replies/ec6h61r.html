    The government being in debt is different from a person being in debt.

    If you want to go on a deep dive into this, check out Modern Monetary Theory. It involves a discussion of deficits that dispels a lot of the myths that come up when people try to use personal debt as a metaphor for government debt. Debt is completely different when you print your own money.

    Stephanie Kelton is an MMT economist who has done a lot of great writing on the subject.
