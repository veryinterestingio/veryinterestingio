      You get so overwhelmed that your body just doesnt know what else to do?

      Rather than thinking of crying in terms of psychological problems, it's probably better to think in terms of evolutionary benefits of expressing extreme feelings. The rest of the group know you're having extreme feelings, and can assist. It may be more beneficial for us to have separate expressions of extreme positive feelings vs. extreme negative feelings (although we already do, to an extent, in other ways, like jumping up and down and so on), but evolution just hasn't developed that in us yet. OR it may be evolutionarily beneficial for us to convey an unclear message: "I'm upset, but I might be happy OR sad. Come find out what's up with me! Oh, and approach me gently, because this could be about anything! (And maybe I even want to have sex with you, and help your genes survive)".
