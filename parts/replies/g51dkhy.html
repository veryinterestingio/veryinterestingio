    To expand on the surface getting dirty thing, most tape is two parts: a strong sticky (what's explained above) that is slow to get sticky, and a weak sticky that is immediate, but weak. The thing is, a little of the weak sticky gets peeled off every time the tape touches something.

    ELI10: The reason tape seems to lose its stick is that the quick stick (tacking agent) usually holds the tape in place while the slow stick (pressure sensitive adhesive) cures. But without that quick stick, nothing is keeping the pressure on the tape, so to speak, so the adhesive won't cure.
