  • tdmeronek

    Paper towel has chemical additives that increase it’s tensile and tear strength when it’s wet, meaning the sheet doesn’t lose it’s integrity as easily after absorbing some water. Depending on the type and amount of chemical, the paper can pretty much absorb its max amount of water and still not weaken significantly.

    Source: did first internship at a paper mill.
