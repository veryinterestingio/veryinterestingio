    Thanks for the informed response. Do you know if active noise canceling earbuds are better/worse/the same at noise canceling as compared to headphones?

    • the-incredible-ape

      Generally an ANC earbud that has a good seal with your ear (i.e. seals your ear from the outside world) should work better than a headphone.

      However, there are a lot of variables. If the earphone's housing is perforated then the headphone might work better. If the headphone has really good ANC performance, it should work better.

      Look for earphones or headphones that claim around 30dB or more of noise cancellation if you are looking for good performance these days.
