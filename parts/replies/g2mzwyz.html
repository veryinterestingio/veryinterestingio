    Recommendations here in the US are to rule out other causes prior to treating a UTI unless there are signs of systemic infection. It makes sense that systemic infection from UTI causes delirium, and those obviously need treatment. Sometimes the UTI gets blamed for the systemic infection though, it's important to rule out pneumonia, skin infections, ect. so that we treat the infection with the right antibiotics.

    In practice, that is hard to do, so patients end up on antibiotics. We certainly need to do a better job.
