    • mfb-

      The eccentricity of Earth's orbit is irrelevant if you measure your local gravitational acceleration, unless you have extremely precise devices. The local gravitational acceleration is dominated by Earth's gravity and your place on Earth which don't change. Tidal forces from the Moon are the next contribution orders of magnitude smaller, which also don't change. Tidal forces from the Sun come next, and they change by a few percent relative to its overall very small value.
