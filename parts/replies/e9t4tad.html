    • TheProfessorO

      Greetings. The Ekman spiral and transport occurs in the upper ocean. The convergence of the divergence of the Ekman transport, due to spatially varying winds, sets up high/low pressure systems that drive a deep geostrophic flow. An interesting fact is that this geostrophic transport is ~ four times larger than the direct Ekman transport using typical mid-latitude values.
