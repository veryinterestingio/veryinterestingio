    • Devildadeo

      They can't. Depending on the focal length you'd like to shoot at, you're looking a around $700 for the mount if you're going to stay below 500mm. Even then you're looking at another couple hundred minimum to actively guide it. Increase the cost to $2000 to reliably track most amateur sized rigs (say 600mm to 2000mm) and add thousands of wasted shots due to uncontrollable variable and now we're talking astrophotography!

      As for telescopes, again focal length comes into play. Cheaper optical designs like f/4 reflectors start at $300 for a 600mm. Refractors that lack chromatic abberation start around $700 for 400mm.
