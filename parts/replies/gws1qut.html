    To add: this mechanism also includes a part that makes us relax afterwards. Ever seen a pet stretch or shake out after a scary event? But because we experience stress so often, these systems get out of sync, get too sensitive. The part of the brain that's responsible for stress response is hyperactive and is physically bigger. Relaxing on purpose, like with yoga, meditation, etc, with time helps the brain part shrink back to normal size and start responding to stressors more appropriately.
