    The most simple way to put it is that a key is just the order the notes are played in. Say you play ABCDEFG, thats A minor. Start on C, so CDEFGAB, and you've got C Major.

    Now if you're wondering what happens if you start on another letter, say BCDEFGA, that would be B locrian. There are 7 different keys or modes and it really comes down to the order of the notes and how far away they are from eachother.

    Its cool to think that you could play the same notes in 7 different orders and invoke 7 different emotional responses, but that's music.
