    I am a dentist. Mandiblular growth is genetically predetermined. Fractures and disease can disrupt the growth, but in healthy individuals, there is determinate growth. Specialized headgear can be used to help assist the maxilla or mandible with accelerated growth, to correct problems of tooth relationship, but the extent of growth achievable is still predetermined.
