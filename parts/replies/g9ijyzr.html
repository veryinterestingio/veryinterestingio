    What is happening is the salt is soaking up the moisture in the steak and bleeding into it, salt specifically is very good at deawing out moisture of veg and meat. Would recommend some salt flakes to season with after cooking and once rested, this will help draw out the natural flavour of the meat and also give you some contrasting flavour.

    Garlic salt where you mince/zest garlic cloves and combine it with salt then leave in a oven at a really low temp to dry back out then blitz ut up in a food processor is one hell of a way to get some good meat seasoning.
