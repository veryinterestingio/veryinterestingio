      • Fishmonger-X

        Spectrum is cheaper at higher frequencies for this exact reason, because with a higher carrier and the same bandwidth you need better hardware, i.e. more expensive hardware, so the spectrum is harder to monetize and therefore less valuable per Hz.

        Companies making a move to higher frequencies regardless have decided the technical challenges are less expensive to solve than the price premium for lower frequency spectrum.
