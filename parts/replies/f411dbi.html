      So is Robertson. All self-centering means is that rotation force can't throw the bit out. Torx and Hex get that award too.

      Robertsons (with good bits) don't slip as easily though and don't require much axial force to keep the bit in.

      Screws can be put on a bit and held sideways easily too, which isn't common for Phillips unless the bit is magnetized.
