    For a little contemporary link, they used ferrets to test reinfection with (human) SARS-CoV -the predecessor to the current coronavirus. Ferrets were used because of the obvious ethical issues with inducing reinfection in human subjects.

    The SARS-CoV ferret model in an infection-challenge study.

    Despite the immune response not being complete, the ferrets had reduced viral loads and the absence of visible respiratory tissue inflammation, as a result of the challenge with the pathogen.
