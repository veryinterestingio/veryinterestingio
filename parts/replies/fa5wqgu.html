    • chesapeake_ripper

      If you a) don't exfoliate already and b) touch your skin and it feels pretty smooth, and c) you don't really have any real acne problems, you probably have the nice, non-sticky skin cells. In my case, if I don't exfoliate my skin at all for a few days - so if I don't apply my salicylic acid/glycolic acid toner once every other day at least - the skin on my nose and chin especially becomes quite bumpy, and if left alone, can transform into blackheads and other zits relatively quickly. If you don't any problems like this, or alternatively, if you suffer from rosaceae or eczema on your face, or just have especially dry skin in general, then I wouldn't necessarily recommend exfoliating.
