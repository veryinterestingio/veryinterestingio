    • Implausibilibuddy

      Flavour and taste are not the same thing. Tastes are perceived by the tongue and cheeks and they only cover 5 tastes: Salt, Sweet, Sour, Bitter and Umami. What most people call flavour is detected by the olfactory bulb in the back of the nose and the receptors there are far more complex and varied than just the 5 tastes of the tongue.

      Salt has a very strong taste but an almost imperceptible flavour (it does have one, but you need to basically snort it, it's so sleight. Think of how a warm cup of salt water smells. Lo-salt has a much more pronounced flavour because it's a different chemical salt).
