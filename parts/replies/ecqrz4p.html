    • BalboaBaggins

      That's more of an in-joke among Chinese communities - southern Chinese people (from the area around Guangzhou) are stereotyped by other Chinese people as willing to kill and eat any sort of animal. Kind of like how Americans would stereotype people from West Virginia as hillbillies or people from Alabama as incestuous rednecks, or whichever regional stereotype you prefer. It's not "We Chinese people all love to consume endangered animal parts." I'd estimate 95-99% of Chinese people have never seen a rhino horn or elephant tusk or had the desire to buy one, but a few percent of 1.3 billion is still a lot of people which is unfortunately what's driving the demand.
