There are no things to exchange heat with. The only energy exchange is your body radiating away it's heat. So if you were naked in outer space but weren't also, you know, immediately dead from a laundry list of other problems, you would not instantly be killed due to temperature changes. You would feel very cold because the only energy exchange would be you losing heat through thermal radiation.
