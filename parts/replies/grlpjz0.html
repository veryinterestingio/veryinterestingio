    • not_falling_down

      Your body can only produce a set amount of histamine at a given time. Scratching triggers the release of more histamine, and can deplete the supply enough to calm the itch for a while.

      Another way of doing that that does not break the skin and cause further damage is to blast the area with a hot hair dryer - not long enough to cause a burn, but just a bit short of that. The itching will stop for hours.
