      Extremely beneficial is right. For livestock it's not even optional. A lamb that gets insufficient or no colostrum is a lamb you are picking up dead off the ground within the day or month.

        The main reason why many people with livestock around here have some colostrum in the freezer. In a pinch it doesn't even matter what species, cow is easiest to get, and a sheep lamb is better off with cow colostrum than none.
