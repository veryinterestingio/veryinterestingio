      This is exactly it. Where I live (in Canada) there was a big news story about grocery stores price-fixing bread a few years back. I don't know it there was any other penalty, but here's the thing:

      They admited they had been involved in this price-fixing scheme with their competitors for fourteen years. I don't know how many gift cards they ended up giving away, but I'm sure it was a slap on the wrist compared to how much money they made over the course of those 14 years. Plus, they were gift cards, so even if it's technically losing them money, people still have to come shop at their store to get anything out of it.

      So yeah, whatever the laws may be, the enforcement is a joke.
