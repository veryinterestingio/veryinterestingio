    • KverEU

      There can be benefit from a hypertrophy standpoint. A stronger muscle is a bigger muscle, usually. At the same time most powerlifting exercises are a skill and not just something that 1:1 relates to raw power, there's a lot of technique and motor unit recruitment happening in a very specific pattern. Opinions (even academic research) still differ greatly on what the perfect way to training for growth is, even though we know a lot more nowadays.
