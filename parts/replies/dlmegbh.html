    • no_life_all_travel

      The generator / alternator on almost all big wind turbines is synchronized with the grid, and is connected to the turbine with a fixed ratio gearbox.

      Since the turbine will spin at a constant rpm, the control system will vary the pitch of the airfoils of the turbine to control the power output.

      Too much pitch and the airfoil will stall, too little and no power will be generated. There is a wind sensor on the turbine that provides input to the control system.

      Very efficient over a wide wind speed. Only needs to shut down in calm or very high gusty winds.
