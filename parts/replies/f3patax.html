    An interesting (but sad) side study is feral children who never heard language during this stage, never really learn to competently speak a language.

    Note that something similar happens with some deaf babies and children as well! Unfortunately people think that its good to force spoken language like English to deaf children even though its not accessible to them (because they are deaf). As mentioned, there is a critical period of time that children need to know language, so if time is wasted on a language not accessible to these children, then they won't be able to learn languages properly in the future.

    What needs to happen is that these Deaf children are exposed and taught their sign language to ensure they have a solid foundation of language while they grow up!

    Disclaimer: I'm hearing but I want to make this issue more well known! Deaf children should be exposed sign language when they are very young to ensure they have a language to grow up into. They can then learn any other languages afterwards.
