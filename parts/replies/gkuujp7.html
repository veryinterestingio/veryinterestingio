• btm109

    It's not even that investors think the company will be worth more in the future. They believe that the companies stock will be worth more which is determined by the number of people betting the price will go up vs the number of people betting the price will go down. There is no direct concrete correlation between how well a company does and the value of its stock. It is all wrapped up in the psychology of the market. People have come to believe that stock prices will (in general) always go up and so they keep putting more and more money into the market which in turn causes stock prices to rise. If anything ever occurs to break this self reinforcing cycle the whole thing will come crashing down as very nearly happened at the begining of the covid crisis when the market lost several years of gains in a short period of time.
