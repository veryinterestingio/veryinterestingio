    Technically, a cartel can be formed to make any kind of agreement that impedes free market mechanisms.

    E.g. a cartel could agree to divide a market regionally in order to give each competitor a quasi-monopoly in their area. They could agree on market standards in order to push a competitor out of business.

    All restaurants in an area could agree to keep closed on Mondays.

    Not all cartels are even necessarily illegal, depending on jurisdiction.
