      An important point for OP’s question is that sleep isn’t binary. It’s not a yes/no question regarding whether something sleeps. It’s a scale, with some things showing sleep-like tendencies. Even plants have been found to do something similar to sleeping.

      There isn’t really a hard line on what does or doesn’t count as sleeping, it’s just natural for life forms to develop patterns in their behaviour and sometimes that manifests as periods of reduced activity.
