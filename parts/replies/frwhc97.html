    • LasagnaMuncher

      Consider that a computer is a general purpose machine that can do many more things. You want to program, get a computer. You want to do graphic design, get a computer. That has a base cost but almost everyone today needs one. You can add functionality to your computer to also play your video games. The premium to upgrade your computer from what you have to being able to play RDR2 at PS4-level settings is less than 250 pounds. It is a matter of adding 100 pounds for a graphics card, 50 for a better cpu, and 25 for a bit more ram. That is the basics of what is necessary. That is how when you look at the totality of a computer, it is a better value.
