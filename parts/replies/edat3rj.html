    You want cool water, not cold water. Anything coming out of a tap is going to be cool, as opposed to ice-water or something out of the refrigerator, which is cold.

    Cold water does more damage. If you don't believe me, do a test by putting one hand under your tap and one hand in a bowl of ice water. You could even burn both of your hands first, and see which one fares better.
