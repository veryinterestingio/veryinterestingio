    • cantab314

      "The Tube" is a name for the London Underground specifically. It's not the formal name but it is used in official communications sometimes.

      Subway systems are uncommon in the UK. There's the London Underground, the Glasgow Subway (which is just one circular line), and the central part of the Tyne and Wear Metro, and that's it. Most major urban areas such as Greater Manchester, the West Midlands, Cardiff, and so on have extensive above-ground rail networks and bus systems, and sometimes trams, but no large underground rail networks.

      In British English the word "subway" more commonly refers to a pedestrian underpass under a road.
