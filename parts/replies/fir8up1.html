    There is no such thing as a double bust. The house will not /can not bust if you bust. Since you are talking about single player odds I presume we are both talking about a single player game. If you mean that on a table with multiple players the dealer can bust and it doesn't change the status of previous losers, that is correct - but that is not a practical or even typical way of looking at or describing the game. The instant you go over 21 your money is already in the rack. The other players will act on their hands and either also bust or stay with something. The dealer is not going to fish your cheques out of the bank and give them back to you if they bust later on when playing their hand against remaining players. It's odd to phrase it that way.
