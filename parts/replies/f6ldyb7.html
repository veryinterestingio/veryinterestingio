    • leSchaf

      That's part of it. It is also due to changes in the salt content of the tears. Initially, tears have a similar salt make-up like the surrounding tissues. But as you continue to cry, the salt content of the tears drops. Prolonged contact with the low-Salt tears causes the surrounding tissue to swell due to osmosis (similar to a gummy bear in tap water).
