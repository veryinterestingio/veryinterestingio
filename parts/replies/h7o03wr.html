    • Muroid

      If it were actually fully simulating a game in all details, it could show you a lot more than just increments by score. It’s much more efficient to just run the numbers for a given outcome than it is to calculate a whole bunch of fine detail that will never be shown.

      They are doing one of two things. Either they are running a bare bones simulation of the game that just calculates how the score is likely to change over time until the end, and then showing you that. Or, personally I think more likely, they are calculating the most likely end result and then retroactively generating a “story” that gets them to that outcome.

      Either way, the full simulation is probably completed almost instantly and the progression it is showing you is purely for effect.
