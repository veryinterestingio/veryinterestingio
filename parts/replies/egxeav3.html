    Much like alcohol promotes certain species to grow, so does fat food (obesity has similar issues and potential treatments), and also so does a healthy diet.

    If you "feed" the bacteria that enjoy healthy food (by eating healthy food), you should promote a beneficial gut biome in the same way that unhealthy food promotes an unhealthy biome.

    This is obviously the slow way though, and on the way you'll be fighting the unhealthy biome, which is actively trying to get you to feed them their favourite food (which is the alcohol in this case).
