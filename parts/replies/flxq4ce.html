      Supplements provide vitamin D. The vitamin is also what's added to milk in many countries.

      That's to make sure the supplementation is useful for everyone who takes it. People who are low on vitamin D tend be those who don't spend much time in the sun, or who live far from the equator where the UV light is weak. Giving them more of the precursor wouldn't help much.
