    a simple parabolic dish that's focusing ambient sound waves into your ear

    An easy visualization:

    A wind scoop, like in the hood of a car. Or a broad funnel in a narrow opening.

    Sound waves that would normally go past are captured and bounce down the scoop/funnel.

    That's what a parabolic dish does, it focuses a physically wider area.

    The added noise is sound that we'd hear in other positions, all being directed into one spot. Hearing the same "sound" from different positions.
