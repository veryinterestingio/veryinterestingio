    More info here: http://chemistry.bd.psu.edu/jircitano/heatcurv.html

    The horizontal axis is energy. Note it requires as much energy to warm the water 100C as it does to melt ice without changing the temperature.

    TLDR: The ice melting is what cools your drink!!

    If you put 32F water into a drink and 32F ice into a drink, the ice would have MUCH more cooling effect, despite same temperature difference. The phase change is primarily what cools it.
