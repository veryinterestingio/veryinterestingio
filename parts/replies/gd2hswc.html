      Yeah it can depend on environmental laws as well. If the refinery flares 100% of the time, they have a base load that is not recovered. Either the emissions cost then don't outweigh the costs of a compressor, or the gas cannot be recovered due to the gas quality (composition). I think mostly it's a financial choice, so if they want to reduce emissions, they can do it quite easily for the base load at least.
