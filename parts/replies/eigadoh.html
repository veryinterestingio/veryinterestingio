      • TheGurw

        Electrochromic film or electrochromic glass. Brand names include Smart Tint, Switch Glass, etc. It's not cheap. Film applied after the fact is typically between $50-120/sqft for the material, labour is on top of that. Glass with the film pre-applied is significantly more, depending on the product you'll see prices between $140-300/sqft for pre-applied film as the interlayer of 2-on-3 laminated glass.
