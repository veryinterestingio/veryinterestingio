  • Ferrum-56

    Small chemistry-related correction: You CAN make a tablet with an ionized drug, but you need to add the opposite ion to balance charge. You don't combine it with a salt, it IS a salt when the drug is ionized and combined with opposite anion/cation.

    Since an ion can never just exist on its own you don't actually add ions together, but for example when you react the base-drug with HCl you ionize the drug and create Cl- as opposite anion at the same time, so the drug is in salt form.
