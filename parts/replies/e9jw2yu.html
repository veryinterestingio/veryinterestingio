    • BellyFloppinChubs

      That’s a good question too. Molecular composition and chain length both would impact segment mobility and how that segment mobility would change with temperature. Chain mobility would ultimately be related to how well the material would flow or lose its mechanical strength.

      Entanglement would play a part, crystallinity (If present) would play a part, intermolecular forces like hydrogen bonding would also factor in. It’s a significant challenge to relate molecular structure to macro-properties but is always an active area of research.
