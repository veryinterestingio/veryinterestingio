    You would feel nothing at all. Just make sure to avoid anything metal or conductive when messing with battery terminals. It is always suggested to not wear any rings when you are touching the terminals. It is not because of the shock through you though. It is because if the metal creates a path from the positive to chassis ground, said metal will heat up a lot. Heat is caused from power dissipation, since metal effectively creates a short that metal is dissipating, in this case 12v*veryhighcurrent (P=IV). You should be careful when removing the leads, but in general touching a car battery is relatively safe.
