  • BrazilianMerkin

    I like the analogy. Hormones and vitamins/nutrients play a crucial role as well. Hormones are the DNA’s foremen telling your body to grow, where, and when. Vitamins/nutrients are the materials needed to actually build (you) the structure. People who are under nourished at crucial ages wind up shorter than they could have been.

    FWIW, there are also other factors at play we don’t have a full understanding of at the moment like epigenetics which are how expressions of the same genes change in real time... kind of like nature vs nurture but significantly more complicated than a ELI5 answer
