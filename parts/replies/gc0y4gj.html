    Not trying to detract from your point at all, but your "familiarity" with your speed is probably mostly to do with the lines painted on the road, which in most countries change length and spacing according to the rated road speed - causing them all to "flash past" at the same relative frequency on roads of differing speed limits.

    For instance, a road with a 100km/hr limit will have much longer, much further spaced lane divider lines than a road rated for 40km/hr.

    Rather than becoming familiar with the speed of your surroundings whizzing by, your brain is more likely "tuned" to the "beat" of the road lines.
