    • iarlandt

      I think regardless of what happens to 'tornado alley', you will continue to see Tornados. When gulf air pushes northward and runs into cold fronts pushing east, you will have the moist gulf air interacting and getting lifted by the higher density cold dry air behind the cold front. Combine that with speed and directional shear as a result of this interaction and you will still have favorable formation parameters being met in many places.
