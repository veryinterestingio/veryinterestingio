      Some people are citing Abraham Lincoln and poets but I don't think they understand that at the time they were beginning to benefit from a somewhat modern lifestyle.

      In 2018 if you have severe depression you can claim disability and continue to eat and survive.

      In the 1600s and all evolutionary points prior, if you had depression and didn't get out of bed to hunt then you or your family may starve to death.

      Depression is actively selected against.

      Depression is leftover from an unsatisfied reward center, normally triggered by successful hunts/eating/mating. Now these things are nearly a given in modern society. With this pressure off of life you then have the foundation to set yourself up for negative thought patterns. The more negative you think the more negative your brain becomes, like making a path through a field of grass. This is the basis of CBT, which aims to weaken negative thought patterns and strengthen positive ones until it is on autopilot.

      Anxiety is leftover from an unstimulated fight or flight response. Normally triggered by wild animal or other humans being a threat to you. This caution is mostly removed in a modern society. As a result this evolutionary advantage of worrying/thinking ahead now appears when there is no real danger.

      We aren't built for the privilege of modern life that makes up such a small part of the human timeline.
