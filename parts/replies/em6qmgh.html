      It’s easier to manufacture plywood to an exact size than using unfinished wood. Sheet materials are usually within 0.25mm at worst.

      With timber, you can get different dimensions on the same piece if you measure it at different times especially if its warm and humid one day then the weather goes cold in the same week. Warping is also a huge problem especially for longer lengths.
