    Space in a black hole forms a kind of duality. There is space in the black hole and a dual space on the surface of the black hole. Everything inside of the black hole is encoded on the surface of the black hole. This is called the holographic principle and PBS Spacetime just made a video on that.

    In any case talking about the singularity inside the black hole is difficult. Essentially the singularity is a point where our math stops making sense. Think about dividing by 0, thats a singularity mathematically. Our current models of gravity and quantum theory simply cannot explain what this is exactly. Many physicists believe that a singularity itself cannot exist in reality and that it is simply an artifact of our models and that better models will resolve the problem with the singularity.
