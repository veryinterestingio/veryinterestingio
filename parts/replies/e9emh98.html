    Okay, so about the cigarette thing... smoking can very easily cause a flash (ignite any vapors in the area). A puddle of fuel won’t catch fire but a flash is enough to cause first and second degree burns. The flash point of gasoline is -40 while diesel is ~120f (so unless you’re in the desert in the summer, not much to worry about there).

    https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Flash_point
