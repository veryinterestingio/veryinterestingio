  • GiantQuokka

    Your hair is constantly cycling between growing, not growing and falling out. When it's long, the amount it grows on a daily basis is pretty small and longer hairs have a tendency to fall out, so it doesn't seem like it's growing. When you cut it short, it grows a lot more compared to how long it is.

    Shaving may cause hair to grow more if you get irritation, though. The irritation causes increased blood flow, which triggers growth and bypasses your body's signals to grow hair since they just increase blood flow to the hair. This is what rogaine does.
