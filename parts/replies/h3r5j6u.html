    The magma is also very high in silica which makes it much less viscous (much thicker and harder to erupt).

    Quick nitpick: high silica content makes magma more viscous, not less. Remember, viscosity measures a fluid's rate of resistance to deformation. Less viscous magmas are your mafic magmas.
