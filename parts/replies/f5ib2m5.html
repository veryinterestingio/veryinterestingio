    I'm not a networking expert, but there are 3 tiers of internet providers.

    Tier 1s are the big guys - they are the ones that are building the undersea cables.

    Tier 2s are in between - they hook in to the Tier 1 providers.

    Tier 3s are your smaller providers, and the ones that hook up the end user and allow them to access the higher tiers.

    So you would want to start at tier 3. You'll have to register with the FCC.
