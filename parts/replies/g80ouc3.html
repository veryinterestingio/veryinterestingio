    one of the early applications of steam was for precisely this problem.

    There is a famous painting by Turner called The Fighting Temeraire hanging in the National Gallery in London which covers an early example of this. It's a paddle steam tug pulling one of the ships which fought a the Battle of Trafalgar to be broken up in 1838.

    It's not a technically accurate painting of the event, but is very close.
