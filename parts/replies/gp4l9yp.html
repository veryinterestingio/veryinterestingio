    What you are describing is stiffness/strength per amount of material, which is true. But adding more material to fill in the space will make it more rigid/strong.

    An I-beam is very rigid but if you fill in the empty spaces between the “I” it will be even more rigid.

    Likewise, if you fill in the space in a convex shape, it will be even more rigid.
