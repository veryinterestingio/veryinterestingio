    A good example of this- fear and excitement might have exactly the same physiological parameters- butterflies in the stomach, racing heartbeat- but the meaning of them is different.

    Much of that meaning is created by what we focus on- the danger or the pleasure of the roller coaster, for example.

    If we're aware of this we can choose what we focus on, and what it means, which gives us a degree of choice about the emotions we experience.
