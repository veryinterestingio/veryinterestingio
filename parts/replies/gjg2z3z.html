    Great Britain is the island in which England, Scotland and Wales sit. That's why the full name of the UK is "The United Kingdom of Great Britain and Northern Ireland".

    The Commonwealth of Nations is an organisation that loosely ties former territories of the British Empire together. The Queen is the head of the Commonwealth. It's more like a club than anything - there are no laws or guidelines passed down by the Commonwealth in the same way as, for example, the EU or UN.
