    • dazzlebreak

      Tectonic plates are chunks of the litosphere which act independently. Some of them are huge, like the Eurasian Plate, some are much smaller. Moreover, most of them have continental crust, but some don't, like the Nazca Plate, which takes up part of the Pacific and currently subducts over the Southamerican Plate, causing Chile to have enormous copper deposits and really strong earthquakes.

      If all goes according to plan, after 50 - 100 million years, the Pacific Ocean is going to disappear and Asia is going to collide with the Americas.
