    This doesn't make sense, since the planets gradually formed.

    In other words, Earth was at some point, a collection of rocks and dust. And then it was a denser collection of rocks and dust and gradually it became a solid collection of rocks and dust, and gradually, the core melted from pressure and gradually, the crust cooled and gradually, the rough oblique spheroid shape formed...

    And at the same time, all the planets were gradually doing similar things.

    So there is no event where "BANG" and then the Earth is formed and then later "BANG" and Mars is formed... etc
