        That energy was stored in the earth at its formation as rotational kinetic energy, since it is rotating. It was rotating at the beginning because, like the rest of the solar system, it formed out of a collapsing gas cloud which had some initial angular momentum that had to be conserved. Slowly, that energy is being lost and going into the tides and also the potential energy of the moon, so the earth is now rotating slower than it once was. It's not that the earth is consistently generating new energy, it's just skimming some off the top of what it already has.
