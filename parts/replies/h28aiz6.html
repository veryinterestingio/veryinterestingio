    • tyronethugbigum

      I spent my teenage years in a “whiplash” style choir. What we often did to practice diaphragm activation was to lay down flat on your back and place a book on your stomach. The aim was to displace the book higher from its resting state as you breathed in “pushing the air down to your stomach.” I thought it was all useless to till we heard the difference in clarity in our voices. There’s a clip of Harry Styles belting notes and on his back. The biggest difference being your mind muscle connection to your breath. Once your master your diaphragm it expands the length of your phrasing, gives you the ability to let out just enough air to hit different timbres of your voice.
