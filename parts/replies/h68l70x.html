      Regarding tremors. I would assume the following (happy to be corrected if wrong) Antipsychotics can cause Parkinsonianism (symptoms of Parkinson’s) by blocking dopamine in the nigrostriatial pathway. In Parkinson’s there is too little dopamine in the nigrostriatal pathway of the brain.

      Cannabis can cause psychosis. Psychosis is caused by an increase in dopamine in the mesolimic pathway.

      Thus I would assume that an increase in dopamine from cannabis can help alleviate symptoms of Parkinson’s. Nonetheless I would advise anyone with Parkinson’s to see their neurologist for management of the disease and not to use cannabis unless advised by their doctor.
