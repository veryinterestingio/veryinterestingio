      • Siphyre

        Waitresses are being paid like 2.17 an hour where I live. And they likely work part time. You are likely paying like $9-10/hr on average for your wait staff for the day. Another 7.25/hr or w/e min wage is for your dishwasher and maybe 45-80/hr for your cooks (buffet). So if your operating hours (simplifying this as cooks may get in earlier to prep the kitchen or something) are from like 10am to 10pm (12 hours) you have about 1200 a day tops but likely closer to 720 a day in wages. So you are really looking at about 20-30k a month in wages for an average size buffet place. Rent on a place big enough for a buffet around here could be upwards of 3-4k a month.

        I have no idea how cheap the food can get because I am researched out, but I imagine it is the cheapest stuff possible based on what I have eaten. If I had to guess I'd say about 1-5k a day is spent on ingredients depending on what day of the week it is.
