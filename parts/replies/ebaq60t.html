      FM Radio exists in between band 1 & 3, as [CH 2-6]-[FM]-[CH 7-13]. Channel 6's audio ends at 87.75Mhz and FM's lowest station begins at 88Mhz.

      In fact some naughty TV stations used to use 87.7 or 87.9 FM as a zombie radio station double dipping on their FCC license.

      Inversely, if you have a wide range tuner like this you could listen in to the audio of local TV stations. Some old handhelds could go a little below 87.9 too.
