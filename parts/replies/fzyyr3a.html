    Your first paragraph describes something called “long term potentiation” which is a generalized increase in sensitivity of a recently used pathway.

    Your second paragraph describes a “schema”, which is a network of related information that your brain has organized. Increased activity for any of these pieces of information can cause further long-term potentiation throughout the whole network. This is often, to my understanding, the goal of word association.
