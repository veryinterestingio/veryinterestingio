    Have you had the flu? Im not sure I would call that response measured.

    To add to that, there are times when the immune system is triggered by something like the flu to go considerably over the top. This often results in what's called hypercytokinemia, also known as a "cytokine storm," when the immune system starts attacking the body's own tissues. It's tons of fun. I speak from experience.

    Reactions like this are believe to be what killed a lot of victims of the 1918 flu pandemic, especially many young healthy adults who died.
