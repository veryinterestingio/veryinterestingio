  • leSchaf

    Some scientists believe that the itching effect that histamine has is not just a byproduct but actually intended. Histamine is initially low and increases only towards the end of healing (fresh scabs don't tend to itch). The itching could be a trigger to signal that the healing process is almost finished and that the now useless scab can be removed. The timing of this process, however, is not super accurate which is why wound often itch before it is safe to remove the scab.
