  • GivesNoShts

    Grass and other plants only take in water during photosynthesis. This is of course while the sun is up. It is best to water before sunrise. Watering after sunset can, like you said, cause more fungus. Another thing to consider is the heat taking the moisture out of the soil through evaporation overnight before the plants and grass can use it. Ive explained this to customers who insist on watering at night (because its more convenient for them) as it being like me going in and throwing water in their face once they are asleep. They arent drinking any of it. And the grass doesnt jump up yelling WTF, man. Otherwise, with the magnifying glass myth, you are spot on.
