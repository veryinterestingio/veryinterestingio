      Geez, that’s a ton!! Just in case you weren’t already aware (with that many differing allergies you probably are). Anaphylaxis is an inflammatory response that occurs in the bloodstream, which causes dangerously low blood pressure as well as a host of other issues. Essentially all food allergies won’t cause anaphylaxis with the exception of peanuts. This is due to the fact that the antigen isn’t able to penetrate the organs of the GI tract. That is not to say they aren’t dangerous, a swelling pharynx can kill just as easily as anything else but (as far as I am aware) anaphylaxis will not occur with other food allergies
