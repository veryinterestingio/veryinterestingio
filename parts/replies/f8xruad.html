      What this means is that if a is not an integer multiple of e then the problem doesn’t have a maximum.

      This is incorrect. There is always a maximum product. The relevant function to maximize is f(N) = (a/N)^(N) over the positive integers. The function f(N) is increasing on (0, a/e] and decreasing on [a/e, inf). So if a/e is not an integer, one of the two closest integers to a/e does give the maximum.
