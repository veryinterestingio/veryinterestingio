    reverse repo

    Its a form of loaning money with stock as security.

    This what the Fed says:

    What are the reverse repurchase agreement operations (RRPs) conducted by the Desk?

    a transaction in which the Desk sells a security to an eligible counterparty with an agreement to repurchase that same security at a specified price at a specific time in the future. The difference between the sale price and the repurchase price, together with the length of time between the sale and purchase, implies a rate of interest paid by the Federal Reserve on the transaction.
