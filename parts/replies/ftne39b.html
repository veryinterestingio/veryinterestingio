      You are still on Reddit. :)

      That said, the posters above have given you really good background info. The drums are usually key when it comes to understanding the time of a song. Part of it is the meter - 4/4 time is most common in popular music, but 6/8 is pretty common too. 3/4 time is like a waltz - 1 2 3, 1 2 3. Part of it is also how fast or slow the song is...beats per minute. Trance music has a lot of beats per minute, while a slow blues song has low bpm.

      It's easy to hear in older songs...listen to the drums on a Beatles song, for instance.
