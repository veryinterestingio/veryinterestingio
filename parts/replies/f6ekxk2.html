      Bernoulli's law is only accurate when you assume that a flow is incompressible (also has to be inviscid, steady, and irrotational). Past a Mach number of about 0.3, the incompressible assumption falls apart, so you can't use Bernoulli's for supersonic flow (Mach > 1).
