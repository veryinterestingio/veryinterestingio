    • ATLL2112

      Basically no sports book has a minimum bet that would prevent you from doing this.

      As others said, you'll just lose money.

      Sportsbooks aren't idiots. They have guys on the payroll that use analytics to initially set the lines. There's no way they'd set lines internally that would let you place bets in a way that would guarantee profit.

      Now, there's times where you could bet 3/4 outcomes and profit if any of those occurred.

      Playoff series: bet 7 game series to be completed in 5, 6, and 7 games might give you good enough odds to profit if any of those 3 outcomes occur. But you'll never see a way to bet every outcome and still profit. Not on the same sportsbook.

      You may be able to do so on multiple books. This is called "arbitrage betting".
