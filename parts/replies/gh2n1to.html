    To add to this, stimulants still have the same effect on the body whether you have ADHD or not - including boosted dopamine levels. The result is different because it boosts ADHD brains to normal levels of dopamine, and non-ADHD brains to above normal levels (causing anxiety/agitation if too high). This can lead to strange situations such as the ADHD mind being totally calm but the body going haywire because it's still experiencing the increased heart rate, sweating, lack of appetite, etc.

    More anecdotally: I rarely drink coffee because by the time the physical stimulant effects wear off enough for me to stop feeling nauseated, the energy/focus boost are also gone so there is no net benefit from it. My medication is extended release so even if the initial side-effects are bad, I'll have the rest of the day with a better brain chemical balance.
