  • CunningHamSlawedYou

    Yes, you're absolutely correct!

    The brain always thinks in images. That's the only way it knows how to think. Words are just symbols, which are just images too.

    Comparative studies have been done on literate and illiterate people, and language seems to change the structures of our thoughts.

    Literate people would for example group a saw, a hammer, a log and an axe into two groups: tools and material.

    Illiterate people would rather group axe, saw and log into one group and hammer into a second group.
