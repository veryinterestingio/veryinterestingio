      There is something called entrainment that tells your biological circadian clock what time it is. Sunlight is one of the reset button. Mealtimes are another. You can also take melatonin. These things come into play when you fly across the country and change time zones.

      Artificial light also doesn't replace sunlight completely; it's different wavelengths and intensities. They do have special sun lamps that mimic sunlight, but most ordinary lights aren't built to do that.
