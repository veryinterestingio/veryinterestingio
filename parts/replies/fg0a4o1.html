    It’s pretty simple to understand, if only one person in the world has a certain virus, that person can cough in 200 people their faces and the R0 will be 200. Does the same person with the same virus get quarantined before he does so and is unable to infect anyone? the R0 is 0.

    So the R0 is based for a huge part on human behavior. And then a small part is about how contagious a virus is.
