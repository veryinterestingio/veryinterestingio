    I have suspected TMJ/TMD - I go through cycles of a lot of clicking and my jaw getting stuck on bad days, and painful until release. You can often get relief with a bunch of exercises though, so i'd recommend searching youtube for TMJ exercises. I can generally keep it at bay with those, just struggle in the cold months sometimes with tensing in the cold.

    TMJ/TMD is dysfunction of the temporomandibular joint (bit of your jaw). I would hazard you've either got it or at risk of something a bit off about the joint there that needs loosening or mobilising a bit. Tell your dentist about it next time you see them, but they will basically start you with exercises so you might as well just do that first.

    The shoulder is a bit more normal, and if it's not causing pain I wouldn't be that worried, but a bit of stretching/mobility sometimes alleviates it if it annoys you.
