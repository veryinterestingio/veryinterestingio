    Expanding on this - 16 is merely the most common leaf size for large books. The main division is always four - so a children's picture book might be 16, 20, or 24 pages - but never 21 or 15.

    The front and last page of these sorts of books are also often glued to the cover - thus the book will be 22, 18, or 14 pages as two of the pages are used to attach the book to the cover.
