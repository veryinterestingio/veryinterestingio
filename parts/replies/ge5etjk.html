    • Monguce

      It's a lot to do with the nucleation sites as per twink above. The little bubbles that you shake into the drink ride back to the top so there are fewer of them in the bulk of the liquid and so there are fewer nucleation sites. This makes it harder for the co2 to escape.

      Also, since they rise, there are more at the top where the gas can escape with out pushing the liquid out first as it would have to if they were at the bottom.
