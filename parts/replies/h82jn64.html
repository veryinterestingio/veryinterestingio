  • DrZaye

    In regards to slow charging being better for the battery, while it is true, it doesn't tell the whole story. Generally, fast charging degrades batteries quicker, but a big part of that is because of the heat generated. Heat is one of the worst things you can do for batteries, the hotter you let them get, the quicker they degrade. Fast charging produces alot of heat in the battery. If you had a way to avoid the heat, let's say by keep the phone on a cold surface or having the plug take the heat instead of the phone (I know OnePlus Dash charge used to do this, idk about other options), then fast charging isn't nearly as bad. Still slightly degrades it, but not enough to notice over a very long time. Source: Owned a OnePlus 5 for more than 4 years, fast charged it all the time between 20-80, battery life was nearly perfect 4 years later
