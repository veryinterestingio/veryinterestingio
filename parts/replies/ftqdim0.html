  • prnRN

    Eh, I’ve never heard the term “tired”, but it’s more like your vein gets irritated. It’s still a functional IV site but it hurts so we’re happy to move it so you’re more comfortable. A lot of medications can irritate your veins and give a burning sensation. Sometimes those medications and fluids can also infiltrate and actually be pumping into the area around your vein rather than into it and that is another reason it would hurt and you’d need another IV site. Hope this helps you understand! :)
