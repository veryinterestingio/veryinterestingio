      • enderjaca

        Correct, unless you use some emulsifiers. Personally, I prefer a traditional cocoa recipe -- dark cocoa powder, some sugar & salt. Mix with a small amount of hot water in a hot pan and make a "roux" with a whisk. Then once it's well-mixed & steaming, add milk. Then heat til your preferred temperature. If you let it sit for 20 minutes it might settle some, but you certainly shouldn't let a proper hot cocoa sit for that long cause it'll be both cold and precipitated.
