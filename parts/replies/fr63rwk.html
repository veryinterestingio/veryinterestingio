        This is the simple version:

        The pituitary generates FSH/LH when blood estrogen levels are low (more in a sec to complete that loop).

        The testes produce testosterone and sperm when they see that FSH/LH.

        Aromatase enzyme turns a small % of circulating testosterone into estrogen. Most testosterone is metabolized by the liver however. It is normal and necessary for a male to have a small amount of estrogen. This estrogen-from-testosterone is actually how the body regulates testosterone production.
