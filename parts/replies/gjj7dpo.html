    Here a method I have found luck with: Choose a task that you are very familiar with, the more complicated the better. Think the process to complete that task through, step by step in as much detail as you can.

    Example: say you’re a mechanic and you rebuilt enough transmissions to know it by heart. Think the process through starting with the first nut or bolt you have to remove.

    I rarely make it 5 minutes and never finish the task.
