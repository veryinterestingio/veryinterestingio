      modern nuclear arsenals are a lot of smaller bombs

      For IxBM delivery, I'd state that as:

      • CEP values for modern reentry vehicles (and their boosters) is much better. Under 100m (or thereabouts) instead of 2-10km. The more accurate you are, the more likely to impact really close to the target and the less overkill you need.
      • A dispersal pattern of seven (or more) smaller devices (equidistant from each other) produces more damage along the ground then a single larger nuke. The big nuke just isn't as efficient at putting its energy into destroying things on the ground instead of moving atmosphere around.
      • Cost (smaller packages cost less to launch).
