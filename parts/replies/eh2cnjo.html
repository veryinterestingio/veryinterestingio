  • bhez

    Yes. I mean, it's okay for it, and it will probably last 2 years with acceptable battery life. But you can prolong a li-ion battery several more years if you know what you're doing and are careful.

    These devices mostly use li-ion type batteries or similar chemistry, which have particular charge characteristics.

    If you install accubattery for Android, it will sound an alarm when you're charging and it reaches 80% by default. Read their help info in their app or on their website or for more in depth technical information, see batteryuniversity.com

    Charging from 0 to 100 % gives it 1 cycle of wear, and charging 0 to 80% does only a fifth of that according the the research that has gone into this app.
