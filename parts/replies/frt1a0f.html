  • Psychonominaut

    That last paragraph you wrote is spot on. You cycle certain thoughts (especially when you don't realise it) which becomes the norm for your day to day narrative, and eventually your life. You start to see withdrawing as the most effective way of dealing with stress or problems and then that cycle repeats. Until you've withdrawn so much (over years and without realising) that you find you struggle to connect with your own emotions and other people in general.
