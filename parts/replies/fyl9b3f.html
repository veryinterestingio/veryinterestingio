      Migrate meaning that stars do not stay in the place of birth indefinitely. Tipically, stars may change their speed and direction due to interactions with other stars and star clusters but also they can be kicked by the spiral arms.

      Because of the high precision in the measurement of the velocity of the Gaia mission, it is expected to be found some difference in velocity of the stars by the time the mission has ended (over 10 years), although this difference will be minimal. We can expect the change in velocity to change dramatically in a order of magnitude of millions of years.
