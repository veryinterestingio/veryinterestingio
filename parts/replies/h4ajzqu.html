      It can’t be done. In order to make blood, you actually need to run it through a human body. The human body is an essential part of the “making blood” process. You can make blood cells separately, but to make complete blood outside of the human body would be something so far beyond the reach of current science that it’s laughable.

      I cannot overstate how complex blood is. You are constantly tweaking it inside you to be sure that it’s right. It contains two separate acid/base buffer systems. It contains sugar and nutrients. It contains escort molecules and signal molecules. It contains hormones and toxins. It’s literally the superhighway of your body. How do you grow that in a dish?
