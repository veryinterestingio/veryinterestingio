    • Khaosfury

      The photon takes 0 time to get from A to B because for it, point A and B are the exact same location. The faster you go, the smaller distances seem. At the speed of light, everything is so shortened its a single point. Hence, you aren't taking any "time" to get there, since you're already there.

      For us measuring it though, the distance isn't shortened. It's an 8 minute journey, and we can measure the distance and figure out the speed of the photon to get us the actual time of the journey.
