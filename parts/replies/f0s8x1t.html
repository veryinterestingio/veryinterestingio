      Sounds like Patellar Maltracking. I have this where the knee cap sticks. Essentially the groove your kneecap slides in is misshapen and so it sticks or tries to pop out and then gets snapped back. Can lead to dislocations (particularly in women as they have more flexible tendons and ligaments) so get it checked out.
