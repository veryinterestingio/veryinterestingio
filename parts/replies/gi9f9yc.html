  • the_fungible_man

    There are two different kinds of precession being conflated here. There's axial precession, in which the orientation of the axis of rotation of a planet gradually changes with respect "fixed stars". And then there's apsidal precession, in which the orientation of a planet's orbital ellipse gradually changes with respect to those same stars. One of Einstein's early successes was explaining the magnitude of Mercury's apsidal precession.
