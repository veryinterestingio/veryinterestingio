      In a bee hive, each hexagonal structure is called a cell. Some cells are full of honey, some full of pollen, and some are chambers are used to grow baby bees. The queens need bigger cells because as larva they grow to be bigger than a regular bee larva.

      Normally special large cells are made for queen bees, but if workers want to make a new queen to kill the old one, they can feed ANY larva to grow into a new queen, and they can expand it's cell to make room for the baby queen, in which case it is known as an "emergency cell"
