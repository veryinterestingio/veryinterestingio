      • Sonem95

        Yup, the moon did slow down a little.

        A gravitational assist is like throwing a tennis ball against a speeding truck. When the ball bounces on the windshield, the truck slows down a negligible amount but the ball is launched at a high speed. The kinetic energy lost by one is the same as the energy gained by the other, but because of the difference in mass, the ball gains much more speed than the truck loses.
