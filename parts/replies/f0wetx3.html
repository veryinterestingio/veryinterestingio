    There are multiple reasons why light can not escape a black hole. The gravity is so strong that light would fall back, like a person throwing a ball up into the air on earth. Also due to the massive gravity, spacetime is so curved inside the black hole that there are no paths that lead anywhere that is not inside the black hole.

    Black holes do emit light (Hawking radiation), but it is not escaping the black hole per se, and there is nothing someone inside the event horizon could do to send light out.
