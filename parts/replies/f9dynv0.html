    • Reddit_is_therapy

      It's when the challenge level or difficulty of the task you're performing and your skill level are matched.

      When your skill level is more than the challenge given by the task, you'll get bored eventually.

      When your skill level is under the challenge level of the task, you'll get frustrated.

      When they are evenly matched, you'll enter a state where you just keep doing the task without thinking too much about it and it's the best match for productivity. In popular culture, it's also called 'the zone' or something similar.

      In the flow state, you're thinking about the task, not the how to do task. You are just doing because you already know what to do.

      Read the book Flow for more details. It's a brilliant book!
