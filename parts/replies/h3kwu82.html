    As an interesting aside, there are power plants in development that use the CO2 has an additional power source. Most natural gas plants use the exhaust to heat steam to power another turbine. These new power plants are taking the exhaust, creating supercritical CO2, and using the CO2 itself to power another turbine. Then they plan to either sell the carbon or pump it underground.
