    Best explanation of bridge so far.

    I used one in the mid-2000s when I was traveling for business and needed to use public WiFi. In some places, I could only get signal if I was close to a window. So I put the bridge at the window, and ran a long ethernet cable from it to my laptop. As far as my laptop was concerned, it was on an ordinary wired network. I just had to use a web browser to connect to the bridge and select the WiFi network to use.

    It was great, but the marketing for it was terrible. They didn't really make clear what it actually did, in layman's terms. So until I got it, I wasn't entirely sure it was the right device for my situation.
