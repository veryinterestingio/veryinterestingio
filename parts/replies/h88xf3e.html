    The Holy Roman Empire actually didn't have multiple kingdoms beneath it. There was only one King in the Empire: Bohemia. Which was such a big deal that when the Duke of Brandenburg-Prussia wanted to style himself King, he was forced to style himself King in Prussia to represent the fact that he was only a King outside the Empire.

    If anything it'd be the German Empire that was like that. As it included the Kingdoms of Bavaria, Saxony, & Wurtemberg.
