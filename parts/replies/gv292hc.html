    I used to work for a property management company and you are definitely right. We forecasted a 5 year lifespan and if someone destroyed it beyond what was cleanable they were charged a prorated amount. Same sort of approach for the vinyl plank, but the key difference was time. A good carpet cleaner can work miracles and avoid replacement all together more often than you might think. There isn't a ton you can do for scratched, chipped, or warped vinyl planks beyond replacement.
