    • ink_monkey96

      There’s coldset inks too, but they’re not as colourfast as the heatset inks. They still look as good, but the ink is much more likely to come off on your hands. But another part of the reason industrial print looks better than commercially available printers is the dpi- dots per inch- used. Commercial printers can get up to 2400 dpi, but will likely run much lower resolutions. Like 600 dpi or so. Industrial press plates start out at 1200 dpi these days and can go up to 4000 dpi. A higher dpi count gives you a lot more control over the image you’re reproducing.
