    Under time reversal the magnetic field would be inverted, since the magnetic field would be produced by some current which would be reversed. Since the magnetic field vector and the velocity vector are both reversed the net effect is that the path does not change under time reversal- no need to involve positrons unless you're talking about time reversal on a single particle instead of universally i.e. the whole "positrons could be electrons traveling backwards through time" thing.
