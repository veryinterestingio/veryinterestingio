    And then working along with the melatonin, which helps you regulate WHEN you should fall asleep, you have adenosine. Adenosine is the neurotransmitter (like dopamine, serotonin, adrenaline) that makes you feel tired and tells your brain IF you should sleep. It slowly builds up as the photoreceptors are actively engaged by light, then as you sleep they seep back into your brain and you feel awake/refreshed again. Caffeine blocks adenosine on the brain, keeping you from feeling more tired than you already are.
