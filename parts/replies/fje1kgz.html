    And if it's taking 10s of seconds to load a program, OP has a rotating magnetic disk... Which can do about 100 distinct things per second (I/O operations aka IOPs).

    So the billion things your processor can do per second get squeezed through the eye-of-the-needle that is the 100 things your disk can do in a second, which it now becomes pretty reasonable to imagine the thousands of things your disks needs to do to get that browser open, and why it takes 10s of seconds.
