    • sb_747

      Not quite.

      When you get a job you fill out a W-4.

      The W-4 ask you questions and when filled out properly tells your employer how much to withhold in taxes.

      If you answer accurately then you should be within a few dollars of what you owe per year in tax. When I filled my taxes for last year I have owed like $4.

      Most people have their employer withhold more money so that when they file their taxes they get a big rebate check. They do this because they prefer having the government basically put money in a savings account for them to spend later.

      Also, Every major life event that effects taxes like having a kid or getting married should be reported to the IRS by filling out a new W-4 but a lot of people forget that and when they file their taxes they get money back as the government didn’t know they had a kid.
