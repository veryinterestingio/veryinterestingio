    • trabbaro

      Yeah, but that is such a weird system.

      The two main stars are both sun-sized-ish, orbiting each other. But their orbit is crazy, going from 11AU to 35AU. Then 1/5 of a LY away from both of them is Proxima, orbiting the main two on some crazy half-a-million-year long orbit, and it happens to have a planet in the habitable zone.

      If you're on the confirmed planets in that system, you won't ever see two suns setting. You'll see one sun, very close by, and two very bright stars in the distance near each other.
