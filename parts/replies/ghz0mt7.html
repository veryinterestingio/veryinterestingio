    Would just like to add a few points. Business bank accounts usually have to pay for their transfers and BACS is cheaper than FPS.. although the fees are small it does add up.

    There is also the ability to halt the transaction in some circumstances due to the 3 day clearing time, handy if a mistake was made. With FPS if you make a mistake there isn't much you can do as it's usually cleared instantly and the money is gone.

    FPS is also not necessarily for one off payments either as standing orders use it.
