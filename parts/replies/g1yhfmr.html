      Quite often while the PE firm will maintain ordinary stock ownership, they will leverage up the balance sheet and cashflows of the target firm to take out their own capital as soon as they can restructure the balance sheet.

      A good example of this was what Terra Firma did with EMI, the record company, and ultimately the lender (Citibank) owned the business - but by then TF had got all its own cash out plus some more.
