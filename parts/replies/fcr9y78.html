    All the "elasticity" "distorting bones" etc are misleading. The cycling of muscle fibres is what causes fatigue in this situation, as well as things like static holds in exercise.

    Demonstration: hold arm out in front of you. No great force is applied but fatigue quickly sets in. Continue to hold arm out and observe spasming as muscle failure sets in.
