    • drpeppershaker

      A big part of loot boxes and gambling (which are essentially the same) is the random reward.

      If you got paid out 90% of your money back every time you hit the slot machine button, no one would do it.

      If you got the thing you wanted every time you bought a lootbox, you'd just be shopping.

      The random reward increases likelihood of addiction because it holds back the dopamine and then gives you a big hit every once in a while.
