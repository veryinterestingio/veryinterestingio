      And the smallerr the transistor, the easier it is to switch. This allows us to both reduce energy consumption and heat production.

      Once you reach low enough power consumption, you can package it with a battery and get a useful life out of it, too.

      When you have to worry less about heat, it suddenly becomes viable to package an entire processor in a mostly-sealed case, like a cell phone; or you can put a pretty powerful processor with even a small, light and slim heat sink, like a laptop.
