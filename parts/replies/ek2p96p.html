    A friend of mine was an extra in the Tom Cruise version of War of the Worlds. There's a scene where a mob pushes a car into the river.

    The car had to be modified before the scene: engine, oil tank, anything else that could cause pollution was removed first. Then, as soon as the cameras stopped, divers went into the river to retrieve the car so it could be hauled out of the river as quickly as possible.

    They did multiple takes of that scene, which meant that they had to prepare multiple cars.
