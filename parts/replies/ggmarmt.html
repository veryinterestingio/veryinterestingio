    • RepsOverEverything

      Great thought process. Yes, there are studies that show faster improvement from illness without taking anti-inflammatories, but the difference in time is fairly negligible. Something like recovery from the common cold in 4 days instead of 5. So, the trade off is between 4 bad days versus 5 moderate days in terms of comfort.

      This gets a bit more complicated when talking about something like a swollen ankle. Too much pain and swelling may cause you to limit joint movement to the point where excessive connective tissue will grow around the joint and limit long-term mobility.

      -Fun fact, soon after something like a rotator cuff surgery, passive range of motion exercises are essential for preventing this.
