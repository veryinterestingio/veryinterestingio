    Lighter pigmentation is an evolutionary advantage away from the equator, as skin pigment blocks sunlight and would result in a vitamin D deficiency otherwise. This is why most Africans who's ancestors migrated out of Africa suffer from vitamin D insuffeciancy and because of that, lower calcium absorbtion, and weaker bones. https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/16549493/

    The opposite is true near the equator because the pigmentation protects you from sunburns
