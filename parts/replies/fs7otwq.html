    Aside from that, imagine a factory has a widget assembler. If they hire on a 5 day work week in a 14 day pay period, the factory needs 1 full time employees for 10 days and one part timer for 4 days.

    On a 4 day week, they need 1 full timer for 8 days and one part timer for 6 days. So you get the net effect of creating an extra job, with all other things being equal, so at least a better earning part time job.
