  • MerelyMisha

    I am not a doctor or scientist, but from what I’ve read, caffeine doesn’t cause anxious thoughts (so it doesn’t cause anxiety), but affects your body in much the same way as anxiety: increased heart rate, shaky hands, more alert, etc. If you are already anxious, it can make you more aware of your anxiety (e.g., noticing your increased heart rate, which is due to both caffeine and anxiety), which can then make your anxiety worse.

    If I’m going into a situation where I might be anxious, I go without caffeine. I want to be able to ignore my anxiety, and caffeine makes that tough.
