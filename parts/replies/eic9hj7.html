    • olsonson

      Alcohol has zero order elimination kinetics, meaning your liver processes the same amount regardless of the amount you have consumed. Therefore, the more you drink the smaller the proportion that is being metabolized at any given time and the longer it will take to reach sobriety. This makes alcohol particularly unusual and tricky among drugs, very difficult to predict when you will sober up.
