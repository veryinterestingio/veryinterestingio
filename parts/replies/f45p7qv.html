    How useful are those charter declarations? I mean, aren't they easily sidestepped with workarounds such as two other companies I control each have 49% of the company?

    For that to work you need a way of knowing, for a given company, who are the persons in ultimate control of it, once all the intervening companies have been "removed". Otherwise, those declarations in charters are obiter dictum, or at least useless.
