      • nivlark

        "Momentum" seems like an odd choice of words, but of the microwave background itself. We expect that its spectrum should be isotropic (the same in every direction), but when we measure it we find that it is redshifted in one direction and blueshifted in the opposite direction. Interpreting those as Doppler shifts allows us to determine the Milky Way's velocity with respect to the so-called comoving frame, in which the CMB would appear isotropic.
