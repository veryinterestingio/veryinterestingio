      • Pelusteriano

        The idea is there, but the explanation isn't quite correct. Instead of thinking about a straight line (like this depiction of human evolution), it's better to think of a tree (as shown here).

        Just like you and your siblings are related, mammals and reptiles are related. Both of you have a common ancestor. Reptiles and amphibians are related in the same way. Amphibians and fish are also related. Saying "humans came from fish" implies there's a direct relationship between them. In reality, humans and fish have a common ancestor, but humans don't come directly from fish.

        If you're willing to go as far as possible, you'll end up finding the common ancestor of all life in Earth, named LUCA (Last Universal Common Ancestor).
