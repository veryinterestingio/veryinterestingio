    • Childofglass

      So they choose areas where there would always be some ice accumulation, that would be our ice caps in Antarctica primarily.

      They also use the oxygen isotope ratios to determine how much water was trapped in ice at a given time.

      There’s 3 oxygen isotopes-O16, 17 and 18.

      The more O18 is in a sample of ice, in relation to the other two isotopes tells more or less how warm or cold the earth was. O18 is the heaviest and is evaporated in lower quantities for that reason.
