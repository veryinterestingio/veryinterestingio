    Unintended mold is often a visual indicator that the item has been in the right environment for enough time to develop bacteria. The bacteria is generally not visible but can be more harmful for humans to ingest, so in some cases even if that unintended mold itself wouldn't hurt you, if it has grown it means more harmful bacteria has also had that opportunity.
