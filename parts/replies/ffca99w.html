    In space, if you aren't concerned with recovering the air, you can just use valves that restrict the flow. Seal the lock and either open a valve that vents it to space or open a valve that vents it to internal atmosphere (but not both at once), wait for it to equalize.

    If you want to minimize air losses, you can suck the air out with a vacuum pump (or pre-pumped vacuum bottle) before venting it to space.
