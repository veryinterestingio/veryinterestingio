  • witooZ

    The actual problem are not cookies themselves, but tracking cookies. Non tracking cookies usually just store some settings that you have set - eg. if you switch light/dark mode of a website, it will probably get stored in a cookie. Tracking cookies record basically everything you do on the website and send it to google and facebook.

    Just get an extension (some browsers do it too, if you switch it in settings eg. Opera) to block the tracking cookies and you will be fine. These extensions usually block the cookie consent window too.
