    Imagine running a saw into your hands. If you go into your fingers from the tips and pretend to cut parallel the you will have little resistance. If you tried to cut across your fingers it would be hard as you have to go through all the flesh and bone. The grain of the wood on a not is always perpendicular if you cut to the center.

    ✋? vertical cut = easy.
