    So I take AP bio and we use the terms genetic bottleneck and adaptive radiation interchangeably- do they actually mean the same thing or are we wrong

    • Zillatamer

      They are not the same thing; genetic bottleneck means the loss of genetic diversity from population loss, like cheetahs, mountain gorillas, ect; adaptive radiation is where one species becomes many new species by adapting to exploit new niches, like Darwin's finches that all evolved from one ancestral Finch.
