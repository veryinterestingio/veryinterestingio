    Yeah, this is why there can be materials that are both white and mirror-like, e.g. milk, polished marble, glossy white paint.

    Subsurface scattering is like mist, but much more dense. This is why milk looks white straight out of the carton, but if you dilute it with water it starts looking more and more like cloudy water.
