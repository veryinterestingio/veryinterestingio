    Flavours popular here in NZ may be different to America. Cultural preferences for example. Americans love cherry flavours, Brits like black currant.

    Part of the reason Black Currant isn't that popular in the United States is because growing them commercially was restricted/banned for much of the 20th century due to concerns about spreading disease to other plants.

    It's no longer illegal to grow on a national level but is still restricted in some states, and there is still very little grown domestically.
