      Yes. They break up through a combination of gassing off their ice and other volatiles and gravity of the sun pulling them apart.

      We actually observed Comet Borisov breaking up and fragmenting after its pass by the sun, though this was an interstellar comet, meaning that astronomers calculated that it did not originate in our solar system, but was rather ejected from a different star system millions or even billions of years ago.
