    Another thing which can be a big cause of water getting into abandoned places (at least in colder climates) are burst pipes. It’s why it’s good practice to leave the heating on minimum if you go on holiday and don’t have a house sitter.

    Water in the pipe freezes, expands, bursts the pipe. As soon as the water pressure is enough to overcome the ice, boom, flood.
