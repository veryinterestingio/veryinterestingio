    The defrost will cycle the AC compressor and use it to get rid of the moisture in the air. It actually helps defrost the windows faster and keeps them defrosted.

    When the AC was broken in my bucket, it would take at least ten minutes to start defrosting. Once I got it working that summer, the windows didn't frost at all till the next winter!

    Seriously though, defrost time went down to minutes instead of "still kind of foggy" as I pulled into work.
