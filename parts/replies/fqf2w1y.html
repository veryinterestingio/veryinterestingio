    where the Canadian stations are allowed to substitute their commercials for the American ones when they are playing the same show

    To be clearer - They're not substituting the commercials. They're substituting the entire broadcast.

    So if Global TV Canada is showing Survivor at the same time as CBS, and if CBS is available on the Canadian cable package, then you will get the Global feed even if you're on the CBS channel.

    Where this falls down is when, as you say, the two channels are showing the same show, but not the same episode.

    Or if the American feed gets delayed by a news event or an overtime sports game.
