      Energy can only be converted, not created. If the furniture is at rest then its potential energy is not changing, so no energy is being converted to heat.

      If the furniture is lifted up then dropped, then its potential energy has changed. That difference in potential energy is converted to kinetic energy and then converted to heat and sound as it hits the floor.
