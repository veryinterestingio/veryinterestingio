      Here's the wikipedia page on MDA for reference. In general, it's mostly amplification, it just makes use of a bacteriophage derived polymerase called phi29 which performs this very interesting debranching method for amplification.

      When we sequence DNA/RNA we can't sequence it in a straight line. We have to chop it up into pieces and sequence those pieces. (There are whole molecule approaches not, but there's no guarantee we found a whole genome molecule either way.) A lot of assembly is guess work and we're actively working on techniques to improve assembly of bacteria and viruses derived from the environment.
