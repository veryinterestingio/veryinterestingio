    TLDR: It would take 1.33x10\^32 box fans to cycle the whole Earth's atmosphere in 10 years.

    Yup currently CO2 is at about 400 parts-per-million. Which means that for every 2500 liters of air there are only about 1 liter of CO2 gas.

    Your normal box fan can move about 1000 liters/second.

    Lets just assume that the Earth is flat to make the math a little easier. The City of Toronto has an area of 630km\^2. Most of the air is within 10km of the surface so that leads to a total volume of 6300km\^3. That is 6,300,000,000,000,000l of air or six quadrillion three hundred trillion liters.

    Lets assume we want to move that volume of air thru the CO2 remover in 10 years. You would need about 20,000 box fans.

    That is just for 1 city. How much would you need for the atmosphere? The Earths atmosphere is 4,200,000,000 km\^3. That means you need 133,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000,000 box fans do the whole atmosphere in 10 years.
