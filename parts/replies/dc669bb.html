    This is correct!

    In order to bond to the glass, the surface is treated and acts as an intermediate layer between the glass and the adhesive/sealant (well and reliably) to keep the glass on your car and keep the elements out.

    The lines that go across the back glass are heater elements that defrost the rear glass by heating it just slightly. You can also find this on fixed rear side windows as well.

    Additionally, some cars have heating bands on the wind screen underneath the wipers to help defrost them in frosty conditions.
