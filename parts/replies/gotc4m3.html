    Inflation applies to everybody just the same. Including the super rich. Inflation can occur in just about any economic system when the supply of money increases relative to the population and to the supply of goods and services. This is just as true in socialism or even communism as it is in capitalism. Inflation encourages people to spend money, which IS the economy. If we have deflation, people are incentivized to save instead of spend, decreasing the exchange of goods and services for money. The economy slows down. Most economists believe deflation leads to a downward spiral that will grind the economy to a halt. This is not unique to, or limited to a capitalistic economy. The corruption in unregulated capitalism is a different issue, and the cause of it is not inflation. Reversing inflation won't make greed or wealth inequality suddenly stop.
