  • goNorthYoung

    One thing to keep in mind, though, is that although honeybees make the news more often because of mass die offs, these same pesticides are causing huge problems for native bees and other pollinators (native pollinators being way more effective at pollinating, in many cases).

    So while yes, if every honeybee died, others could pick up the slack, but the real issue is that if the huge die offs we’re seeing keep happening, there won’t BE enough pollinators to pick up that slack.
