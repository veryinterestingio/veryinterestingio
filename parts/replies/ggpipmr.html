      Need food. Chores get food.

      Remember that this current dopamine excess is pretty recent. For most of human history dopamine was quite difficult to get. Even if you were extremely rich and never had to do anything yourself, you'd still find it quite hard to get dopamine because there just wasn't that much to do. Besides sex, anyway, which many religions restricted (and people took religion pretty seriously back then).

      It's also not just about dopamine. It's also about the avoidance of suffering. Your brain only pursues dopamine if it's not experiencing suffering, which means those who are experiencing it are likely to search for a way to avoid it first, meaning get food, safety and so on.
