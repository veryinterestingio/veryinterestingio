    • niceguysociopath

      The pizza is probably because pizza usually tends to dry out as it sits. Mold and bacteria need a certain amount of moisture in order to reproduce and thrive, so drier foods are safer. This is why foods like jerky, honey and rice can last so long, there's not enough moisture for the fungi/bacteria to grow. Pizza tends to be a dry brick after lying out, so bacteria can't do much to it.
