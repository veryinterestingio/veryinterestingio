      Yes, it’s a culmination of In Silico, In Vitro, and In Vivo models to characterize the safety profile. Ideally we’d love to move away from using animals in any research, but we’re just not at that point, still too many unknown variables to contend with. To put it in perspective, we still struggle with differences in how drugs affect and are metabolized in different racial backgrounds. So it’s important to look at the difference between Caucasian vs Asian vs African data, there are metabolic differences between each that can change the kinetics of the drug you’re testing, which lead to different efficacious results.
