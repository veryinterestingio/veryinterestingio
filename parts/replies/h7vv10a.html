  • Vivillon666

    This is called pre-authorization and should be done in times when at the time of paying the exact amount you will be paying is not known. For instance in Europe if you go to selfservice gas pump you first choose how much gas you need, amount gets pre-authorized to assure you have enough funds to pay for it, then you are allowed to get the gas you are pre-authorized for. After you are done the exact amount of money gets charged. If someone is using pre-authorization for transaction when the price is already known I would say it's not a best practice.
