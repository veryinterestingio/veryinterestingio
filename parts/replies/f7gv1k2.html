  • fartandsmile

    I build cisterns for water storage and without any light getting in the tank you don’t get algae etc. The same principle works today as did back when. I have studied traditional water systems throughout the Middle East / India and they usually have a series of sediment traps to keep water clean then a dark tank, often below grade to buffer temps. Many of these systems are still operable with minimal maintenance over hundreds of years.
