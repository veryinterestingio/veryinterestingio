    • jaminfine

      What you're talking about certainly is possible. However, it would no longer be emulation. The key piece that makes something emulation is that you are digitally representing hardware. If you just rewrite the game to run on the hardware you already have, you've translated everything in advance and don't need an emulator. However, that rewriting process may be very involved and likely you'll need access to the source code.
