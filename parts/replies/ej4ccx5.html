    I’d like to add to this:

    If you have a poor posture, don’t try to avoid breathing ‘into chest’ for breathing ‘into stomach’. The rib cage has to move during breathing for all the surrounding muscles to work properly. While this isn’t a problem for people with good posture it can be for those slouched over.

    The way that lets you get the most air in is the right way to breathe. Diaphragm lowering and chest expansion should happen at the same time.
