    I’m one of those people who dislike Fortnite, and I’ve spent some time thinking about “why” I dislike it.

    In the beginning it was fine, just another game with the battle royale genre. But then came all the “extra” stuff in the game that detracts from the genre, be the last person alive.

    Cosmetics are fine, but when there’s a shopping cart with the sole purpose of “make a ramp and ride said shopping cart down the ramp,” that’s when things get too extra. Or trying to build a stadium around a soccer/football field. If you want to build something, go to a game that’s meant for building stuff.

Some of the people who stream it aren’t always the best people, and I know from experience that fans want to emulate their favorite streamer.
