    I think you are using the word "series" when you mean "sequence." In math, a series is when things are added together. The number 0.9999.... can be written as the series from 1 to infinity of 9/10^n. Normally you would write that in sigma notation, but I don't know if you can do that with Reddit markup.

    Part of the reason that this concept is hard to talk about is that defining and understanding what 0.999... really means requires the concept of a mathematical limit, which is a step into Calculus territory and definitely beyond ELI5.
