    • indecisive_maybe

      The size is a big difference. So different colors of light have different wavelengths. Smoke particles are really tiny, and let large wavelength red, orange, yellow light through nicely, compared to other colors that are scattered more.

      Water is more transparent and droplets are a lot bigger, so it actually absorbs those smaller colors, reds etc, and lets the smaller wavelength (higher energy) blue, green, purple through better. For example the ocean is blue-ish, and gets more blue the further you go down.
