    • CoffeePWO

      Not just serotonin. There are different types of anti depressants for a reason. SSRIs (selective serotonin reuptake inhibitor) are common, but SNRIs (Serotonin and norepinephrine reuptake inhibitors) are common too, or tricyclic (serotonin, norepinephrine and dopamine) or MAOI (monoamine oxidase inhibitors). Increasing the uptake of serotonin is very common, but definitely not the only mechanism.
