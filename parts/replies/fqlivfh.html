    This might be a dumb question but why do asthmatics find it more comfortable sleeping face down or mostly facing down?

    • jayaram13

      Your lungs are mostly in the back. So sleeping face down helps the alveoli (tiny air sacs in the lungs) to stay open and drain easily. This helps keep the lungs clear and breathe easier. Incidentally, it helps a little if you have the flu, covid19 or pneumonia for the same reasons.
