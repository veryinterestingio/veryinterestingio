    Med student here. Your urinary tract has 2 circular discs made of muscle that wrap around your urethra. Those are called sphincters, you have 2 for your urethra and 2 for your colon. That's why when you get the urge to use the bathroom you can ignore it, the initial urge is your internal (closer to the bladder) sphincter opening which you have no control over. That is an involuntary muscle. The external sphincter you have control over and thats you not pissing your pants. Long story short your body can ignore the signal its just on you to do it not automatic nervous response. Hope I explained it well enough for yall.
