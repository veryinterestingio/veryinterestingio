    With all the talk about power to liquids and CO2 drawdown, we need the grid to be far cleaner and approximately 3-4 times larger for these strategies to realize any meaningful progress. We also need to figure out the grid-scale storage problem.

    I would like to see all of the different fields combined into one simple energy solution: thermochemical fuel production, with biological material upgrading of the plants byproducts, all combined with the newest battery materials for storing excess energy to be used the following day within the plant.
