    And that's exactly what happens now with online streaming. The ads you see on Youtube (or wherever) are targeted based on the user profile they've been able to build up about you. And by "you" I really mean "your device," because they track the device by an internal "advertiser ID" with the assumption that only one person uses a given device the majority of the time. Logic works well enough for phones and computers, gets a little iffy for something like a TV.

    I don't know if *broadcast television* has that capability via set-top boxes, but I suppose it's possible.

    And yeah, it's pretty complex. I worked in ad tech for almost a decade and I still don't fully understand all the moving pieces involved.
