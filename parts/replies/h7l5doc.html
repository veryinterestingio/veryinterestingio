  • rndrn

    Note that when trying to force a password, 99% of the time it doesn't happen through the online service, instead it happens offline.

    Typically, when a service is breached, the attacker will get access to the database of (hopefully hashed) passwords. The attacker can download this data on his computer and then make as many guess as they want. This is orders of magnitude faster than through the online service.
