    • Zoaea

      Nausea is also a component of your Sympathetic Nervous System, often referred to "fight or flight" in layman terms. Basically when you are getting anxious your body is preparing you to fight or run for your life so it is shunting blood from non essential organs (like your stomach) to your muscles. Your fear and anxiety will make you more nauseated because it will keep triggering your sympathetic nerves. If you google Sympathetic Nervous System you can find a list of other effects it can trigger. The Parasympathetic system is its polar opposite.

      ​

      You could try to find some calming techniques, basically if you can trigger your Parasympathetic system it will counteract the Sympathetic one. GL.
