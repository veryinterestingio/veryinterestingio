      The magnetic field produced by the current is only one factor. You also have vortex lattice flow to deal with. That just depends on the actual current density flowing through the wire, not magnetic fields around it. For this purpose you can sometimes actually want to intentionally add defects into a superconductor to pin the vortex lattice and prevent it from moving (and dissipating energy).
