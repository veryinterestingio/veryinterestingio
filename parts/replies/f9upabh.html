    • jaksblaks

      Spinning disks cap out at about 100MBps. Good quality ssds can reach up to 500MBps on sata or up to 3GBps on pcie.

      There is also a difference between the max speed protocol supports and what the device can actually reach and no ssd can currently max out the pcie bandwidth without implementing raid internally. Most people think that devices can reach the max protocol speed when its just not true.

      Consoles are all sata based and ship with spinning disks. Computers can use either sata or pcie but reaching the theoretical speed requires an optimal use case that is almost nonexistent in real world usage.
