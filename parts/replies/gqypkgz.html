    You also need to accurately "Forecast" your call demand, which is sometimes more art than science.

    There is a lot of science, though. There are companies -Blue Pumpkin comes to mind - that specialise in call centre staffing algorithms, based on your expected call demand. I worked at places that had call stats dating back years, and their ability to predict call volumes was very good.

    But any random event can ruin that planning. An internet outage - such as we've seen sporadically over the last few months - means an almost instant uptick in call volumes, as people can't get to their online banking, for example. Can't predict or plan for those.
