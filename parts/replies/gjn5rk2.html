      • demize95

        Just let Windows Update do its thing, for the majority of drivers. Graphics drivers you may want to periodically check their site or use GeForce Experience to automatically check for updates (for NVIDIA cards; not sure what the equivalent is for AMD cards, but I'm sure there is one) but you'll still usually be fine with just the drivers from Windows Update.

        Of course, if you're having issues with any device, go to the manufacturer's site and get the latest drivers directly from there. Windows Update does lag behind a bit, so if you think a driver update will solve your issue then go straight to the source.
