    3) is really the key here.

    To expand on it a bit further, the idea is that instead of targeting a single keyword (i.e. the name of the recipe), when they write a whole story, the search engine now can match that 1 page with tons and tons of other searches.

    Now a search of something similar to the recipe might land you a spot further down the list on another search, or better yet, in the #1 position of some random search that most people don't usually search for.

    These random, infrequent, search terms are what someone above called the "long tail". It's not worth a lot of effort in targeting them directly, but you can accidentally get some by writing lots of content.
