        It's not too crazy. Your eye just really doesn't care a ton about colors in comparison to contrast. Your brain makes up a lot of the colors you "see" in real life anyway, so there's not much point keeping things exactly right as long as the overall gradient is somewhat realistic. Your brain literally just fills in whatever is missing.

        Watching a video in 4k still has increased brightness detail, though. So it's not just about the color improvements.
