    • Problem119V-0800

      It's probably fine. It only takes a small amount of leakage to interfere with wifi. A typical home base station transmits with 0.1 watt or less; a microwave is pumping 1000 watts or more into the food, so it doesn't take much to stomp on the wifi signal.

      It is possible for a microwave oven to leak dangerously, it just isn't common. If the microwave's door is loose/askew or if it doesn't latch properly, then you might want to get it fixed or replaced. If there's nothing physically/visibly wrong then it's unlikely that there's a leakage problem.
