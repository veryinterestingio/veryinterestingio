    There are different types of surnames in English and an occupational name isn't the most common. The oldest and most common surname are derived from a given name. They may be a first name like William, a patronymic name like Johnson, matronymic like Marriett, or a clan name like O'brien.

    Other types of surnames include place names like towns or geographic locations, like Hamilton or Hill. Another is personal characteristics like Strong.
