  • Harsimaja

    Yea the obstacle is extracting it as pure titanium.

    Rutile (TiO_2) is the most common ore. Carbon can steal the O away if there’s also Cl to distract the Ti, but then you have TiCl_2. Magnesium is most effective for snatching the chloride away to leave pure(r) titanium, but magnesium itself requires its own whole process to extract electrochemically in the first place.

    So it’s like “water water everywhere, but not a drop to drink”.

    But processes improve.
