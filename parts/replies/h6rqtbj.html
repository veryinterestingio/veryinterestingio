Sleep with all the women i ever wanted to sleep with. For a time- it was the greatest thing I’ve ever discovered it was the answer to my laziness.

      I had the confidence of the person I always wanted to be as a nerdy, fat, bullied, girlfriendless, first gen- Indian child.

      QUITTING OPIATES I had been an opiate addict for 15 years (functioning on pills) I wanted to see if I could quit using methamphetamine which was so much stronger than any drug I had ever had in my life. And for time it worked when I tried to quit heroin I stayed up for 14 days on meth, smoking it, I didn’t eat anything I didn’t shower I didn’t sleep I didn’t smoke cigarettes and I didn’t do heroin I don’t even remember if I was sick or withdrawing .

      In the end I needed methamphetamine just open my eyes and I needed heroin just to calm down and feel “normal”

      Fast forward five years and now I’m sober looking back and realizing that I was out of my mind but only now do I realize it- if you would ask me then I would tell you I wasn’t even getting high: the drugs weren’t even working anymore- but they were working.

      I’m diagnosed bipolar 2, they say, medication has been helping me a lot, keeps my moods even-ish. I think I was self medicating my ups and downs or maybe inducing them, hard to self investigate my own disorder.

      Hopefully one day this can be an inspiring chapter of redemption for me. Because if I go out and use again it’s likely I will die, if not immediately- certainly quickly.
