• daveinsurgent

    You need a water heater mixing valve. The water heater should be very hot, not just because it makes it last longer as you said, but it's a health issue. The output of the water heater gets mixed from 'scalding hot' to 'safe hot' as it exits, and then your shower can mix to hot/warm as desired. This is safe, gives you plenty of hot water, good temperature control at the point of use, and a modern water heater is pretty well insulated so it's still quite energy efficient.

    • sonicjesus

      120 is hot enough to prevent bacterial growth. Mixing valves are usually used for undersized water heaters that will run cold prematurely. The hotter they run, the less efficiently they do so and the shorter life of the tank. Also, if the plumbing doesn't have an expansion tank it will damage the plumbing over time from expansion and contraction.
