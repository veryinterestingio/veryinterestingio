      • PussyStapler

        Incorrect. The lung is not a muscle. Most of us are cardiac limited in our exercise. Doing sports will improve your delivery of oxygen by pumping more blood. Your lung function does not change with more training. Exercise improves cardiac function. There is no way to improve the mechanics of breathing for most people. Running and eating healthy will not improve lung mechanics.

        Eating a carb-heavy will improve your respiratory quotient, meaning you produce less CO2, and that could have some effect on breathing. Athletes used to do this in the 80's, but no longer do this because the carb heavy diet doesn't really improve performance, just changes a number
