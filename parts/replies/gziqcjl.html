    Why were there neutrons and protons?

    • the_joy_of_hex

      Because of the strong nuclear force. You probably already know that the electron and its antiparticle the positron have a property we call "electric charge". According to the theory of quantum chromodynamics (QCD), the quarks that make up neutrons and protons have an additional property known as "color charge".

      In the same way that having an electric charge causes particles to experience the electromagnetic force, having color charge causes quarks and antiquarks to experience the strong nuclear force, and this is what is responsible for the quarks binding together to form neutrons and protons.
