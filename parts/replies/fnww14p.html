    What you're describing with threads being lock isn't a race condition, it's called a deadlock. Race conditions is the phenomenon where the execution of competing threads isn't properly planned so the resulting computation is random depending on which thread finishes or goes through its computation first, resulting from simultaneous access of a shared ressource.
