    • mfb-

      It gets redder and darker quickly. For stellar black holes this happens in less than a millisecond (from the time where you start noticing effects), for the most massive black holes you might keep seeing the object for a day or something like that. The typical timescale is the Schwarzschild radius divided by the speed of light, or ~10 microseconds per solar mass.
