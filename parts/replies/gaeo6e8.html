    • ItsSchlieffenTime

      Memories are stored by the connections between our neurons. When we repeatedly think about certain memories, we reinforce the connections between the neurons involved, and make them more likely to fire together again in the future. There is a famous phrase in neuroscience "neurons that fire together wire together".
