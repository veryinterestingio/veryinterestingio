    If I may make a small correction:

    The liquid portion of the blood with clotting factors mixed in is called plasma

    The liquid portion without clotting factors is called serum. Source

    Platelets are not part of the clotting cascade. That belongs to factors 1-12. Platelets are merely a sticky structural component that meshes together to seal a wound. They are activated by TF (tissue factor)

    When you separate blood you can actually see a thin layer of white cells and platelets called the buffy coat.
