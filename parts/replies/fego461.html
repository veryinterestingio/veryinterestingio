    Yep, egg yolk peritonitis. Very common amongst hybrid birds that have been bred for max egg production and nearly always fatal - essentially the egg breaks inside the bird and the nutrient -rich yolk is a perfect growing medium for all sorts of bacteria. Cue massive infection in the peritoneum and usually death. Our girl had a prolapse with an internal broken egg and we had to cull before either infection set in or the others killed her.
