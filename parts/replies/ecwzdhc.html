  • onlytoolisahammer

    One of the biggest fixes is simply removing noise. The problem with multi track recording is that adding each track adds noise. But voice and various instruments only use certain frequencies, so for example you can filter out high and lows from vocal tracks, taking a lot noise with it. In this way you can clean up individual tracks and then mix them back together. Just that alone can hugely improve the quality of sound.
