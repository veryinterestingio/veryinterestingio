  • malastare-

    I read somewhere that the vaccines might also help against other coronaviruses (common cold)

    Please be careful when presenting information like this.

    The "common cold" is not a single disease, but rather a set of different viral infections which are lumped together because they generally have pretty similar symptoms and severities. There are a number of different viruses that can cause the "common cold". The vast majority of common cold infections are caused by Rhinovirus, and it becomes the typifying virus for what a "common cold" is.

    A minority of cases are Coronavirus (which tends to be the more severe colds), with a decent number being Influenza (which, technically would be "the flu", but when mild its hard to tell the difference).

    So, you've got some decent information there, but the common cold is primarily Rhinovirus. The fact that people get colds so often means that for any one of us, there's a good chance that one of the colds we had in the last few years was actually Human Coronavirus, and that's where the Cold/COVID interactions occur.

    Any impact that COVID vaccines would have would only likely reduce the severity of a small portion of colds (that 15% or so that are Coronavirus), while it would have no effect at all on the colds caused by Rhinovirus, Adenovirus, or Influenza.

    EDIT: Just to make sure that I'm clear about my tone here: I'd have made the same request of the authors of the linked articles. Both main stream media and scientific journals have been making references to Coronavirus using phrases like "other Coronavirus infections (common cold)" or "the common cold Coronaviruses". These are technically accurate, but give the impression that "common cold" == "Coronavirus", leading to misconceptions in normal people. It's not even the reader's fault. It's so easy to misread the intentions of the author that you'd practically need to already know the information to parse it correctly. This isn't meant to be a rant against a particular person. I'm just fighting my useless battle to try and clarify the situation.
