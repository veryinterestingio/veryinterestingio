    I started intermittent fasting just over a month ago. I'm 6'1" and was about 235lbs. I WAS a huge foodie and lived in a city with crazy food options. I ate till my food was gone, and often did all you can eat places, though, much healthier than American restaurants for sure. But, these also often included all you can drink (alcohol) as well...

    When I started IF, I struggled hard. I'd eat a little, and needed to eat again soon, or I'd feel like I was crazy starving. This only lasted a week though. After that I was able to keep to two meals within a 7-hour window. I only drink water, and if I have cereal, it's with almond milk.

    Today though, I'm now completely fine eating one normal size meal around noon. And that's it. I've even increased my output, but I wouldn't call it a full on exercise routine, but I'm definitely burning something, as I can see it on my body and face. But, the hunger isn't ever really there!

    This explanation really hit home for me, as I never thought I'd hit this point when I started. I have only ever "cheated" once a few days ago by eating 30 minutes before my normal window.
