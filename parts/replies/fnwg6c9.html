    QuickTime requirements, DirectShow filters that are missing (codecs), or requiring older versions of .dll's. Any of these can break videos or music in older games.

    For example, Monopoly Tycoon, Test Drive 6 and Ford Racing 2 encounter stuttering music that skips all over the place when version 6.05 of a system component (.dll) is present in Windows. Whereas version 6.03 of the .dll makes the music operate properly, however it also requires a removed component to function.

    Later versions of Windows Media Player changed how the files were played, as they are now translated by a newer system in Windows. This new implementation causes the music to stutter in only these three games.
