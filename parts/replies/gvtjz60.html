    Is malaria a parasite?

    • TheStatIsALie

      Yes, malaria is a parasitic disease caused by the genus Plasmodium which are obligate parasites of vertebrates and insects. As far as human health, it's generally considered the primary parasite of concern.

      Yes it is! Malaria is the disease caused by plasmodium falciparum. It is more specifically a protozoan that lives inside liver or red blood cells (depending on life cycle). Since it is a unicellular eukaryote, it is small enough to live inside our cells and evade our immune surveillance.
