    Before locomotion, there was no movement to food and so the interesting world was shapeless. Afterwards, sensory organs developed at the end facing the direction of travel, front and back were born and the world was a tubular nub. And with it left and right, and the interesting world became a sphere of danger for some, and a cone of attack for others.
