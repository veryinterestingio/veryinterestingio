    • bennynthejetsss

      A slight vaginal odor is natural and healthy, as it is with just about every other part of the body that is not constantly exposed to air. However, “odor” does not equate with “bad.” A vagina and vulva should NOT smell foul (like something rotting) or fishy. That indicates an overgrowth of harmful bacteria that can cause irritation, itching, pain, and inflammation.
