  • JudeOutlaw

    Umami is pretty much exclusive to glutamate and its infamous cousin that got a bad rap for no reason other than probably racism: monosodium glutamate (MSG).

    You can buy pure MSG at the grocery store as a seasoning called “Accent.” I highly recommend it. Adding it to your food is practically a cheat code.

    It is only umami. Like how sugar is only sweet and salt is only salty.

    If you buy some, pour some in your palm and eat it by itself. It’s a trip your first time. It just tastes like... umami. You’ll get it after that.
