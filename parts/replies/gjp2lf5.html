  • iridisss

    Lot of factors: what kind of panel the monitor uses (IPS/VA/TN), how hard it is to produce a high refresh rate display, and how many units they expect to sell (4k TVs sell like hot cakes compared to gaming monitors). Monitors also typically have much less input lag. If you've ever tried to play a game on KB+M on a TV, you'd likely notice a significant delay compared to monitors. Not so noticeable on consoles and controllers with their deadzones and dampened input, but much more noticeable with a mouse's direct inputs.
