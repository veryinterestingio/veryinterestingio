    Typically it happens when you are lifting very heavy, or right towards the end of fatigue. If you are lifting things that are easy, you'll never get any shudder.

    It typically also happens vastly more in compound lifts, as a lot of the time the "shudder" is due to compensation and balance (as this is where the errors in recruitment are most likely to happen as explained in top post). You're much less likely to get a shudder on machine lifts.
