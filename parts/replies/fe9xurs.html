  • ZAFJB

    Many many years ago I travelled from Patras to Athens on a Greek train made up of about 6 DMUs.

    That has a three speed manual non-syncromesh transmission. Imagine a gear selector rod running down 6 cars...

    Train would pull away in first, hit peak revs, driver then attempted to double declutch into second, crunch, crunch, grind. Train would lose so much momentum driver would have go back into first. Repeat several times, eventually into second. Peak revs in second, repeat again going into third. Quite amusing.

    Explains why we were about 4 hours late into Athens.
