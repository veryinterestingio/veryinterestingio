    • d3vrandom

      it means it's a separate entity and the owners are only liable to the extent of their investment i.e. their share in the company. it's a warning to potential creditors.

      sole proprietorships are another type of business where the owner bears unlimited liability towards the debts of the business i.e. even his house can be sold to pay off the debts of the business. partnerships are another example of this. the partners personal assets can also be taken over to pay the debts of the business. so the inc., ltd or plc. tags on the names of businesses are there to distinguish them from sole proprietorships and partnerships.
