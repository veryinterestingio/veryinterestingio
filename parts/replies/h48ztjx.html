      You basically have just described a phase change in matter. They occur whenever there is a discontinuity in a thermodynamic variable (first order phase changes, ie solids, liquids, gasses) or a discontinuity in any nth derivative of a thermodynamic variable (higher order phase changes, ie superfluids, superconductors, plasmas, ect). However, within the phases themselves, everything should be continuous.
