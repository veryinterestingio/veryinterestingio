    • PurityOfEssenceBrah

      Oceanography major here: lidar won't work, the laser wavelength does not penetrate water effectively. Sonar or a fathometer does very well at determining depth, especially when paired with an XBT (expendable bathythermography) which allows us to measure temperature (some expensive ones also do salinity. These help with sound speed modeling for the top part of the ocean. Ocean depths and alternatively topography can be estimated using gravity measurements from satellites.
