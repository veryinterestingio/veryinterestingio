    • DoctorPooPooHead

      Depending on shape or size, red birthmarks may just be how your blood vessels formed. A red birthmark may just be that your superficial or skin deep blood vessels formed in that way. If you have had the same birthmark for years or since birth, it's very likely that it's not a big deal. But it's also a good idea to talk with a dermatologist to make sure. Especially if it just appeared.
