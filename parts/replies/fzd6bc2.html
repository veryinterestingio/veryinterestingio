  • MrsFoober

    I worked at a junkyard/recycling place (Wertstoff Hof in German) last year for a month and a half.

    It's not just straws that fly away like that. Styrofoam is the worst. People pack it in these weak ass bags and they rip apart way too quickly. Then you have the styrofoam flying around EVERYWHERE.

    All these little things, you can bet your ass that that shit won't be recycled properly. It flys around everywhere and I'm pretty sure it looks like that on most of these recycling places.

    Where I worked it was on a tiny island. The dumping ground was right by the ocean. So much flew over the tiny brick wall and the seagulls are everywhere picking apart trash and scattering everything even more.

    If you want to make sure everything gets recycled properly, do it yourself. That applies to pretty much everything.

    Everything we let other people take care of, is not taken seriously in a lot of instances.

    If you want something to be done "right"/your way, you're better off figuring out how to do it yourself.
