    Just to clarify, the flu vaccine is less effective because the flu changes so much year to year. If we waited until flu season to tailor the vaccine to that particular strain, it would be too late to produce and distribute enough vaccine. So the vaccines need to be made in advance based on educated guesses about what the next strain will be like. If the flu didn’t change year to year the vaccine’s efficacy would be >90%
