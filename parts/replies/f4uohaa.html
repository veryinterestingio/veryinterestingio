I use beta blockers when the symptoms last a bit longer, sliding also happens the most when my stomach is empty, with full stomach there is feeling of pressure. Burbing has never been so gratifying as it usually means that the pressure around the stomach lining opening that links esophagus to the stomach, the place that has given away, is relieved.. so it is a REAL relief, heart rate goes down, nausea goes away, adrenaline secretion slows down...

    It is apparently quite common, one fifth will get it but most show no symptoms.. but knowing what i know now, i have had it for a LONG time... So. if anyone reading has weird panic attacks that are triggered by really nothing, if heart rate goes up with no explanation, you get the feeling of "dread" that goes away on its own... talk to your doctor.
