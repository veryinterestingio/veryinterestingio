• ADHD_Broductions

    I would like to add that the way exponents are explained in school is unintuitive and wrong.

    The way it was explained to me was 'how many times you multiply a number by itself'

    2^3 = 2×2×2×2 because you multiply three times, right?

    In reality, as long as we ignore non-integers as exponents, the way it should be explained is 'how many times you multiply 1 by the number'.

    2^3 = 1×2×2×2

    2^0 = 1

    Intuitive.
