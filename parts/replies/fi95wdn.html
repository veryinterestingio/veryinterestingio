      They don't require massive amounts of electricity.

      They're built using superconducting electromagnets. The materials used to make the wire for the magnet are only superconducting at very low temperatures, hence the need for liquid helium cooling.

      Once the magnet is "energized" at installation, this initial electricity will flow with basically 0 resistance in the wire indefinitely, without any external power source.
