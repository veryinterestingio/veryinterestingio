      Stopping from proper cardio will be only due to your body running out of energy (out of carbs and/or fat). Heart or lungs will never be the limiting factor in a healthy individual.

Lungs not keeping up is unheard of as far as I know, and heart can theoretically go up to 240bmp if necessary before hitting a wall (absolute max heart rate, basically impossible to reach through exercise without the muscles completely fatiguing first)
