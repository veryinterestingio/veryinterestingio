    Basically for fear of a seizure, ask your neurologist what the chance of you having another one will be. After around 5 years without one, you're down to less than 10%.

    Just avoid things that increase your chance of a seizure - alcohol, lack of sleep, grapefruit (grapefruit interacts with pretty much every medication around) and accept that if you have a seizure, you are gonna feel like crap at minimum - just try to alert someone if you can.

    or at least lose consciousness safely (I usually lean forward if I feel one coming on) - better to break the nose that slam my back on something
