    Just for clarity

    Lower wavelength means a shorter wavelength and higher energy

    Higher wavelength means a longer wavelength and lower energy

    So for example, it might absorb light with a wavelength of 300 nanometers (shorter and higher energy, ultraviolet so we can't see it) and emit 500 nanometers (longer and lower energy, and visible)
