    • StarFaerie

      Yes. All out of the skin just different lengths.

      You can see it in medium and long haired cats if you part the hair. The coat looks glossy and slick when patted down but as soon as you part it you see there is lots of fine fluffy hair under that top coat at a shorter length so it doesn't show normally. It's even a often different colour from the guard hairs on top. My boy has black areas where his undercoat is gray.
