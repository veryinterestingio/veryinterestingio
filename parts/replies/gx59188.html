    • TallyHobe

      22 years of service in the Belgian Army.

      Couting bullets was always considered a no go. You don't have time for counting bullets when s**t hits the fan. We always put 5 tracers in a 30 5.56mm rounds magazine when tracers are available (yay for budget cuts).

      But in general it's a question of click and no bang equals shouting reload (getting cover form a buddy) and then going through a reload drill .. or a faulty gun drill.
