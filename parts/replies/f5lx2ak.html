      Music composition major here. My personal recommendation is to go through some beginning piano lesson book series, as they progress through different common and ‘classic’ chord/harmonic theory from the most basic and build up as you go through the books.

      It will give you a sense of what works and you will gain some intuition on putting together your own. My 11 year old has been taking piano for around 2 years now and is just kind of picking up composing by mimicking the concepts in those books alone and playing with it.
