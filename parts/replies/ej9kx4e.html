    How do you manage your vEDS?

    • hardrocker943

      I’ve only recently found out I have it so I’m still working on that. But with my variety I just have to try and limit strenuous activity. With vEDS, it’s in my organs and arteries, so I could die tomorrow from a random rupture. Or I could die 20 years from now.

      But for the most part, it’s a combination of specific exercises, building in days to recover during the week, vitamins and supplements along with lots of meds to keep my blood pressure a bit lower and just trying to live with it. There’s no cure, so it’s all supportive. I have to have all my teeth removed in a few months because it’s caused my teeth to die from the inside out. It affects so much of my body. Just gotta accept it and not give up.

      Edit: Just for context, I’m 29. I have the joints of someone twice my age.
