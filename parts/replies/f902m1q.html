    • Astromike23

      how does the coriolis effect play any effect with the mushroom cloud?

      It doesn't.

      The Coriolis effect acts on large scale atmospheric and oceanic structures that have a considerable difference in latitude between north and south edges. This is also the reason why the whole "toilets flush the opposite way in the southern hemisphere" is bunk.

      A mushroom cloud is far too small for the Coriolis force to have any appreciable effect on its motion.
