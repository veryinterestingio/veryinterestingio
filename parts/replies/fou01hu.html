      There are 5 types of shock:

      Hypovolemic- lack of blood or volume. (Blood Loss)

      Septic Shock- severe infection dilating blood vessels.

      Neurogenic- spinal cord related that dilates blood vessels preventing effective blood movement. (Spinal or brain injury)

      Cariogenic- heart muscle death or blockage of effective heart beats preventing the heart fr moving blood effectively. (Heart Attack, Pneumothorax)

      Anaphylactic shock- allergic reaction that dilates blood vessels preventing effective blood movement.
