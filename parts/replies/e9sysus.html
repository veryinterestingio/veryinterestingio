      An easy way to look at this is to lay out a rope, extension cord, whatever, with one end looped back to meet the other end. Stand on one end and pull the other towards you. The point where the rope loops around towards you moves only half as far as you pull because you are pulling from the TOTAL length of rope, not just from you to where it loops back around.
