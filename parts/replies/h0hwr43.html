    Yeah I think this phenomenon is actually under represented in these comments. Technology is still constantly advancing, but the journalism about it is specifically designed to drive traffic, not to report on the actual progress.

    By the time it has gone through the countless trials of rigorous science it is old news, and so it quietly just becomes another treatment option. (Assuming it is viable of course.)

    If you look at cancer survival rates over the last 30 years it is very obvious that cancer treatments are getting much better.
