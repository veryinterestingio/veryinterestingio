• Therese_bird_brain

    Great question! This is something that a lot of labs are looking into. It seems that if birds are stressed due to limited food or shelter when they are young, they can fail to learn song properly and their song will be less attractive to other healthier birds. Birds in urban areas have also been known to increase the pitch of their songs so that they can be heard above ambient noise. Climate change has different effects on different habitats and birds, though, so we can't make any general claims about ALL birds.
