      Depends on the accusation. Some stuff is difficult to prove, but other examples of war crimes have been so blatant that even a nominal victor is asked to explain how the hell this was ever permitted.

There are networks of allies and trade partners and international media presence. It’s possible to argue that “what I did was not technically a war crime, so I shouldn’t be punished for war crimes”, but it’s very very hard to pretend you never did anything at all.
