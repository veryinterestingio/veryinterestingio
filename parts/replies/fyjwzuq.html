    To add to this - it’s called bioluminescence when it happens in living organisms like fireflies, glow worms, and many marine species. It’s called chemiluminescence when it happens in a glow stick, which includes a glass vial of hydrogen peroxide that you break into a solution of dye and other chemicals.
