    • dhslax88

      Topical adhesive patches follow the 6-18-end of patch rule. Typically patches won’t start to take effect until 6 hours after application, will reach peak/steady state around 18 hours, and continue to deliver that steady state for 72-120 hours (3-5 days). Keep in mind that heat will increase absorption of the medication, so hot tubs, saunas, or fevers will cause increased/rapid absorption of the drug. All in all, patches are not a great option unless you have a self limited issue (for instance a 3 day scopolamine patch for a short cruise).
