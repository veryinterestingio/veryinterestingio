    Consider a closed environment with two adults and a growing child:

    One adult says "bananas" when they express anger/frustration/surprise. The other adult says "fuck". Both adults are otherwise similar in their appearance, demeanor, and their treatment of the child. In other words, the child has no bias between the two adults.     Which word will the child copy? Will the child exclaim "bananas" when they stub their toe against the doorway? Or will the child say "fuck" when they hit their shin on the low table in the living room?
