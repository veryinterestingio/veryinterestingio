• WorshipNickOfferman

    Local fancy steakhouse was advertising 24oz, 45 day dry aged steaks for $100.00, so my brother and I decided to go. His steak was a beautiful purple color when cut into while mine was bright red. His had that deep, mellow, mushroomy/cheesy flavor that comes with an aged steak. Mine tasted like steak.

    We showed the difference to the waiter and he argued with me, saying they were both the same aged cut. The “chef” (who was a sous chef) comes out and says the same thing. Bartender overhears us and joins the conversation. Takes one look at my bright red steak and starts going into hemoglobin and the aging process and how a properly aged steak would not be a bright red color on the inside. Then the real chef/owner comes out and looses his shit because the waiter and sous chef argued with me about a steak that clearly wasn’t what they advertised. Owner replaced both our steaks and comped the meal. Bartender got a huge tip, didn’t leave the waiter much.
