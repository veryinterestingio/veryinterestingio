        There are two different things at play here. I'm not aware of any evidence that SOD affects the misfolding of PrP. However, SOD itself can misfold and propagate in a prion-like fashion. Such SOD aggregates appear to play a role in certain forms of amyotrophic lateral sclerosis. See Wright et al., 2019 for a review.
