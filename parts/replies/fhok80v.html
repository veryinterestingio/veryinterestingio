    • internetboyfriend666

      Yes. Candidates can transfer funds to a campaign for one office to another. It's quite common when a candidate seeks higher office. The only exception is for a candidate who is running for 2 offices at the same time. So for example, a member of the House of Representatives who is running for reelection and running for president at the same time can't transfer funds between the 2 campaigns, but they can transfer funds if they decline to run for reelection in the house, or if they want to run for president in the next election cycle.
