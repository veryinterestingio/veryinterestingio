      • milktearelax

        True, I live in Japan and love steak, so I eat wagyu pretty regularly. Normal supermarket wagyu steaks (those are non-graded) already taste fantastic and are much more tender than most other beef in the normal supermarket category. A small (~90g) low level wagyu steak starts at around 6-7 USD equivalent.

        The higher the grade, the more fat. A5 (most expensive) is so fatty, that some people think it tastes gross. For me, it's comparable to foie gras. You'll likely only want a small piece of that and eat something else as well.

        Personally, I'd recommend slightly more expensive supermarket pieces (e.g. 16 usd for 120g) or lower level in the restaurant (A1-A2) if someone tries it for the first time.
