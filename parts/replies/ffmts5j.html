      • squeakershoes

        High Functioning Autistic here. I've tried noise canceling headphones, earplugs, or earbuds with noise canceling built in, and they help a little. In those cases, I just try to deal with it.

        However, when the noise is too loud for those tools to help, I simply need to move myself to a quiet space for a few minutes. Some of these quiet places include the outside of a store, or a restroom.

        If none of those places help, or if other factors are built upon the noise, I will need to find an escape from the stressful area, possibly abandoning my plans so that I, and everyone else, is safe. I know, having to stop what you're doing stinks, but sometimes, safety is better.
