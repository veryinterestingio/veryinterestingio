    One problem with negative mass is that a gas made of positive and negative mass particles has no ground state. Since kinetic energy: E = mv^2 is always negative for negative mass particles, for any given state one can think of a lower energy state by giving the negative mass particles a higher velocity. When computing the dynamics, the positive and negative mass particles can accelerate to arbitrarily high velocities without violating conservation of energy.
