      Jumping on to clarify about gout. When Uric acid crystalizes in joint spaces they form sharp needle like shapes that trigger an intense inflammatory response mediated by neutrophils. Our first like treatment for gout is actually NSAIDs like indomethacin but steroids can be used in severe cases to tone down the neutrophilic response. Steroids in generally are very powerful anti-inflammatory drugs because they inhibit the production of cytokines that trigger the immune response.
