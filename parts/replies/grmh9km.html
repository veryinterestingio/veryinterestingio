      FYI, amnesia like you'd see in movies where a person just suddenly forgets big of their life and who they are is incredibly rare (although I've seen a case in the ER!), and almost always psychological.

      Far more common is amnesia that involves a period of time where you can't form any new memories, usually due to drug intoxication (alcohol), head trauma, stroke, etc. Also, Alzheimer's and other forms of dementia cause a slow loss of past memories, ability to form new memories, and general functions of the mind.
