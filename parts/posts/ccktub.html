    5_on_the_floor A:

    It's genetic. Next time you're at the family reunion, notice how everyone is kind of shaped the same. Some people are predisposed to carry more fat around the mid section (apple shape), and some people are predisposed to carry more fat in the hips and (pear shape).

    There are other variations, and not everyone fits exactly into one category or the other, but you'll notice that people who are related tend to be of the same body type. There's nothing you can do about this genetic predisposition, other than just not carrying extra fat.
