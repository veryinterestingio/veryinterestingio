    [ELI5] Why does the suction wind from the back of the fan feels weaker than the wind blown out by from the front of the fan?

    _corsarius_ A:

    The back end of the fan is drawing air from all around it, while the front is pushing air in a sort of 'beam', so the actual volume of air that the back moves is the same as the front despite its lower velocity. If you were to build a cylinder that comes out of the back of the fan then the air would move at roughly the same speed as the front.
