    woodbetween A:

    When things bond together, they enter a lower energy state.

    To make them break, you have to do work on them (push them, pull them, cut them, etc.). This temporarily increases their energy so that the bonds can break.

    Now that the bonds are broken, they have excess energy and settle back into a lower, unbonded energy condition. To settle, they release their excess energy, which comes in the form of sound waves.
