    Knuspel A:

    As far as i know it depends.Between Macau and China there's huge bridges that loop to the other roadside, but there is also land crossings where you "just switch". https://photorator.com/photos/images/the-bridge-between-china-and-macau-that-cars-switch-from-left-to-right-hand-drive--29847.jpg

    Between the UK and Europe there's no land crossing so therefore there is no switch needed, you just drive on the other side once you arrive in the other country.

    If you need more info, there's a great wikipedia page: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Left-\_and\_right-hand\_traffic
