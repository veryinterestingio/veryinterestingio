    ELI5: Why Richard Branson and Jeff Bezos both had space flights of only several minutes? What limited them to such short time periods instead of a longer more impressive space tourism “vacation”?

    PassingJudgement68 A:

    Their rocket and space ship were only designed to just get into space but not stay in space. In order to stay in an orbit, a space ship has to accelerate to 22,000 mph (or around there). This requires a massive amount of fuel. Neither of these ships were designed for this. They just carried enough fuel to get into space and once there, they immediately begin to fall back to earth. Neither of the ships will ever be more than a really expensive roller coaster ride.
