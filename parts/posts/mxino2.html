    illogictc A:

    Well, half-life is just when about half of something has decayed into something else, but you don't literally have to wait for the halfway point to know it, you can just observe how much has decayed over X amount of time and do some math to expand that to the halfway point.

    MikuEmpowered A:

    Half life is the time it takes for HALF the material to degrade into the lower element.

    This isn't a alarm clock situation where once the time is up, half the material instantaneously turn into a different material. it happens over time, and we can measure the very very tiny amount to extrapolate.
