    Spuzzell A:

    As a former child actor on stage, I can also say that the impact of a scene on the viewer is utterly different to the impact it has on the actors.

    Good actors and a strong script will generate fear and emotion in the actors during the scene, but because you rehearse everything so many times over and over - starting from everyone messing up cues and missing marks so it's more funny/annoying than scary - and because you're hanging out for weeks with the other cast members outside of the scenes it's really not remotely as impactful on the actors as the audience.
