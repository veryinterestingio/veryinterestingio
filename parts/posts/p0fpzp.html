    tampering A:

    As people have noted, you cannot change a formulation or the drug substance without going through a new clinical trial.

    The biggest changes from the clinical trials and the approved drug monograph to this point have been 'off label' dosing regimens. Some countries have extended the gap between the two doses (to 2-3 months vs 3-4 weeks) in order to give some protection to a greater proportion of the population in the short term given supply constraints. There has also been dose mixing (heterlogous prime-boost) and some countries are preparing to give 3rd doses to at least some of the population without waiting for clinical trial data.
