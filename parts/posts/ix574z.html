    tezoatlipoca A:

    Its expensive. The reason Wagyu is marbled aka fattier is its lazy. The cows stand around and are fed constantly. Cows eat A LOT... and its high quality feed too. == $$. Meanwhile, your average beef cow is kept out on the grasslands. Grass = cheap.

    In Japan, Wagyu might be the way to go because of scarcity of pasture/grassland vs. land costs, but everywhere else its much more cost effective to let cattle graze. Wagyu cows are also allowed to get older and fatter - ~3 yrs as opposed to a year and a half before slaughter for a regular beef cow. So your Wagyu crop basically takes twice as long to mature.
