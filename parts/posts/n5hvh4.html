    stcat35 A:

    Volcano in Iceland erupts. The eruption releases a ton of ash containing all those bad chemicals. Those chemicals and the ash reflect the suns rays and heat back to towards the sun resulting in climate unable to properly grow crops. Little to no sun light reached the earth during that period, with records of historians claiming year long darkness. Such harsh climate conditions led to incredibly poor crops and thus massive famines across Europe, middle east, and China.

    Also had some bubonic plague outbreak thrown in shortly after this time in Roman Empire which hastened the fall of it.
