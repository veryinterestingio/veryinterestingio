    Oclure A:

    I've seen guitar center and musician's friend do this. Both companies are owned by the same group and some of their products are special versions made for them and thus have a unique sku number. The day before one site puts an item on sale the other will raise the price. And as the sku is unique to their two websites if you try to do a price comparison by searching the sku online you will always see the sister website as your top result giving you a false sense of the items value. They advertise these items in limited time sales creating a sense of urgency in hopes that most customers won't have time to do more in depth research into the product.
