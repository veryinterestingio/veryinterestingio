    kyrokip A:

    It feels good because of the hormonal/dopamine rush from the physiological response. The pop you hear is nitrogen gasses escaping the joint when you change the pressure. Why Some pops are louder or not, depends on the size of the joint and location.

    Regarding If it's bad or not, as long as you don't hyper-extend the joint or go outside the normal range of motion, you are fine. For example, for your fingers don't bend them back towards your wrist. Instead close them and make a fist. If you go past the normal range you risk ligament damag or loosening of the ligament.
