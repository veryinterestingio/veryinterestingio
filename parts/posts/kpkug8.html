    central_marrow A:

    When you do a self service transfer to another account here in the UK it’s cleared by a system called Faster Payments which is usually instantaneous (but not guaranteed to be). FP operates 24/7. FP is designed for self service, one off, transactions from one account to one other account.

    Payroll payments are usually done via an older clearing system called BACS. BACS takes 3 working days to clear and does not clear on weekends. The advantage of BACS for the sending side is that it’s one to many: one debit from their accounts, multiple recipients.
