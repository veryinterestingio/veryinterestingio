    Oznog99 A:

    Photosynthesis requires at least blue and a little deep red (green is actually thrown back unused).

    Firelight is 1700K-2000k, very skewed towards red and infrared. There is very little blue. You might cook your plants with infrared heat before making enough blue at that color temp.
