    Dr_Singh_ A:

    Change in the structural complex at a molecular level in B-complex vitamins vs. Vit.C. Whereas you find B-complexes in similar foods like cruciferous vegetables, you will only find Vit.C in the most acidic foods also because Vit.C itself is an acid i.e Ascorbic Acid.

    Also, B-complex vitamins are all water soluble but endophillic vitamins that readily mix in our body tissues and stay there for months and years and also function as very important co-factors (helpers) in our teensy tiny (but very important) bodily functions

    Whereas, Vit -C is mostly an antioxidant. It kills harmful free-radicals generated from our daily metabolism in cells ( much like smoke coming out of cars). So here Vit.C functions like a catalytic converter, in purifying this smoke to some level. Vit.C also helps in our wound healing and plays an important role in making our very fibers (ligaments and tendons) of our body. Vit.C is a hydrophilic compound which means it loves water and you lose it as you lose/refiltrate your body water. This is why you need to replenish your body's Vit.C levels almost every few days. Because of these very big and obvious differences in the functioning and mechanisms of these vitamins, they are paired separately.

    Edit: Vit.B are water soluble
