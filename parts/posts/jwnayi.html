    Q:

    ELI5: Why is one nostril blocked while the other is unblocked when breathing through your nose?

    Izalias A:

    It's all about maintenance.

    Temperature regulation and immune response.

    Your lungs need to keep to a good temperature, and the nose can get quite cold. So to keep things regular, one side blocks up to gain temperature, and the other cools down.

    This is why it is worse in colder environments.
