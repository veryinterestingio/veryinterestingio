    Q:

    Eli5: Why do movies or TV shows on television play at one volume, but when commercials come on, they play at higher or ridiculous volumes?

    darkice83 A:

    It's called dynamic range. Movies spend most of their time in low to medium volume so the loud parts gave a bigger emotional impact. Commercials are as loud as possible to catch your attention. Look into the loudness wars for more info
