    tdscanuck A:

    The mechanism inside mechanical clocks the regulates their speed (and hence time) is called an “escapement”. There are many designs but they all have a common design of releasing one tooth on a gear (“tick”) and then allowing the gear to turn exactly one tooth then catching it again (“tock”).

    The sounds are the escapement catching and releasing the teeth.
