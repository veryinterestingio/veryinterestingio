• Biology Why do some people get more bug bites than other people when they’ve been in the same area for the same amount of time?

    NEW
    Q:

    Multiple times on a hike, swim, etc, I’ve always gotten at least 5+ more bug bites than anyone else (lots of different people) on almost any outdoor activity.

    What gives? Why do bugs like me so much?

    DiamondIceNS A:

    Insects are acutely sensitive to pheromones. That is, smell. The simple fact of the matter is that everyone smells different, and insects have favorite foods.

    I guess you just smell delicious.
