    ELI5:How amd why dark circles appear around our eyes when we don't sleep enough?

    joaofelipenp A:

    The skin in the region around our eyes is thinner and more transparent than the rest of the skin in our body. So, depending on your skin color, we can see a bit of the blood vessels that are behind it.

    The vessels usually are small and not visible through the skin. However, two things happen when we do not sleep enough that changes that:

    First, the body does not drain enough liquids and substances from our blood, as it usually do when we sleep. Since there are more liquids than usual, the vessels need to be larger and more visible.

    Second, the body produces things to keep ourselves alert and awake. These things have the side effect of enlarging blood vessels, which also make them more visible. When we rest properly, we do not need these to be awake.
