    As previously described, sinuses are lined with erectile tissue.

    However, they also respond by swelling and inflammation to allergens, especially in the very early morning hours.

    Antihistamines, the 24 hour kind, taken before bed will help. A good air cleaner with a HEPA filter will also help.
