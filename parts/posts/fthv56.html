    Q:

    If poachers dig into the face of the animal to take away the horn. Why not take the whole skull with you?

    fakeman17lovesu A:

    As others have stated, rhino horns have no real medicinal value. They are made mostly of keratin, which is materially similar to fingernails and quite unlike bone. In an alternate reality, traditional superstitions would be leading poachers to take the skull and leave the horn.

    As a side note, it has recently become possible to produce fake rhino horn that is also made of keratin and is virtually indistinguishable from the real thing. Some have proposed flooding the market with fake rhino horn as a way to drop the price and reduce the incentives for poaching.
