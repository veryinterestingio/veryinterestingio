• Biology If a human is limping, are animals able to tell the human is injured?

    NEW
    Q:

    For example say someone hurt their leg and is limping to walk, are birds, cats, dogs, etc able to pick up that the person is hurt?

    parkinglotfreestyle A:

    Not sure about animals as a whole, but it is proven that predatory animals will preferentially target injured/weak/sick animals as prey. Some argue that predators actually do not target any prey in particular and they simply tend to catch the weakened ones, but most professionals agree on the former.

    Obviously some animals are more intelligent than others, but I would have to say yes for a lot of "smarter" animals! Dogs certainly pick up on human emotion so I see no reason why they couldn't recognize an injury if its reasonably obvious
