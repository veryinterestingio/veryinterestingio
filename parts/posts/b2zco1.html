• Physics Why do towels that are washed and then air dried end up so much rougher than ones that are tumble dried?

    NEW
    Q:

    But when I dry myself with a towel, and leave that to air dry, it ends up being soft again! What's going on?

    xvier A:

    The fibers stay clumped together and form little points when air drying, rather than being fluffed apart in the dryer.
