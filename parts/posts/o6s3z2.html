    Q:

    ELI5: Why do people began to feel nauseous when they haven't eaten enough throughout the day?

    GCS_3 A:

    In order for your body to get nutrients out of food it needs to break it down into smaller and simpler pieces. Your stomach makes a strong acid to help with this, but it's a bad idea to make it all the time because that acid can damage the stomach. Because of this, your body will try to predict when you usually eat and start making this acid beforehand so that it's ready for the food.

    If you go a long time without eating when you normally would, this excess acid can irritate your stomach and esophagus which then causes nausea and discomfort.

    Sipping water can help dilute the acid and will also help prevent dehydration (we get a lot of water from our food).
