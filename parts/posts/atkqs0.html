    billified A:

    Electricity works on demand, not by pushing like water. All the way back to the generator at the power plant, electricity is in the line just waiting for something to say "I need some juice!" Until then, electrical energy sits in the lines as electrons bouncing back and forth between molecules, ready to move again when needed.
