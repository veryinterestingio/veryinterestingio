    Disenthalus A:

    First: NEVER TRY TO PUT OUT A GREASE FIRE WITH WATER. Oil repels waters, so putting water on a grease fire will cause the grease to violently spread. Grease fires are best extinguished by smothering. So shut off the stove and put a metal lid over the flames to cut off the supply of oxygen. Salt or sand can do the same but you need a lot of it to smother so using salt will only work on a very small grease fire.
