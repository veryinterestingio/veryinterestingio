    Q:

    ELI5: Why and how do ingrown nails happen?

    jithinsarath A:

    When you trim your nails and you cut it in such a way that the new growth can gradually cut into flesh, ingrown nails happen. They are also caused by wearing shoes that are tighter than normal, by pressing the sides of toes into the skin and a over a period turns into ingrown nails. There are other factors like injury, irregular growth of nails etc. but the above two are the main ones.
