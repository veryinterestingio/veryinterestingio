    There are some pretty bad answers in this thread ?. Guys I’m pretty sure OP knows what they are & what causes them, he’s asking why they happen.

    In short, we dont really know why they occur. We barely know why SSRIs work in the first place, and a lot of the withdrawal symptoms are an even bigger mystery. One theory is that discontinuation of these drugs leads to temporarily low GABA levels. Since low GABA has been liked to increased seizure risk, it is postulated that these brain zaps might be small, localised seizures.

    We do know that despite ther discomfort, they’re usually harmless and in almost all cases disappear.
