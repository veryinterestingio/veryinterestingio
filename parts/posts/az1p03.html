    LurkerSwede A:

    You might be interested in this article from 2017 examining the Triviers-Willard Hypothesis (TWH) from 1973 for a specific population of chinese farmers. TWH states that socioeconomic status influences sex ratios. Its introduction section points out papers that have previously examined this in human as well as non-human populations.

    Other factors have been claimed to affect this (testicular temperature etc), though I have yet to see peer reviewed results substantiating such claims.
