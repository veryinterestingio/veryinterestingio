    NEW
    Q:

    ELI5: How did popcorn become a standard movie "meal"?

    Away-Reading A:

    Street vendors used to sell popcorn outside theaters during the Great Depression. It was dirt cheap to make and sold for only 5 cents a bag - which made it one of the few treats that cash-strapped people could afford. Eventually, theaters started selling popcorn themselves.
