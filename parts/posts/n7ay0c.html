    Q:

    Would dominoes collapse faster on a planet with stronger gravity than Earths?

    RobusEtCeleritas A:

    A collapsing domino is like an inverted physical pendulum toppling. The timescale of the fall is on the order of the period of the pendulum, which goes like 1/sqrt(g).

    So yes, the time it takes to fall is shorter if g is larger.
