    Q:

    ELI5: Why if I go to sleep drunk, I wake up hungover even after 12 hours of sleep, but if I sober up before sleeping I'm fine?

    jezreelite A:

    Alcohol is removed from the body through the digestive system and metabolism. Both of these slow down when someone is asleep, which means that the alcohol is not removed as quickly or efficiently as it would when they're awake.

    Alcohol also interferes with the quality of sleep, which makes hangovers worse.
