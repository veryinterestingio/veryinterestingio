    skallskitar A:

    Mostly yes.

    The fat reserves contains lots of energy. However we need to get certain nutrients that cannot be produced from fat alone. Beyond a certain point everyone dies, fat or not.

    If we instead give the persons a very limited amount of food the nutrients can keep both alive for longer, with the fat person likely living much longer than the slim one.
