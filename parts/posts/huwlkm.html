    rsclient A:

    One of the hidden reason (this is a bit IMHO): so many types of fraud are only possible when there's a delay in transactions. It gives the bank computers some time to detect transaction patterns and make decisions about whether fraud is likely or not.

    With very very fast transactions, suddenly that security blanket is gone: an account can be drained of money in an instant, and the money vanished into a maze of accounts, never to be seen again.
