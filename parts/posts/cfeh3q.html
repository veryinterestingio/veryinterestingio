    Comewhatevermaycry4 A:

    A lowered brow can show disapproval and a raised eye brow can mean surprised or shock. When we only raise one eye brow we are in between these expressions and it allows the recipient to offer clarity in the situation so we can settle on one emotion. If our species has found that this works in the past we will continue doing it to show others we are unsure how to feel.
