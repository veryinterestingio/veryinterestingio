    Jon_the_Human A:

    One explanation I haven’t seen here yet has to do with the orbicularis oris oculi muscles.

    These muscles surround your eye sockets and help you keep your eyes open. If you spend long amounts of time with your eyes open and concentrating, like when reading or using the computer, the orbicularis oris oculi muscles can become fatigued to the point where it looks as though you have bags under your eyes.

    Edit: it is oculi instead of oris
