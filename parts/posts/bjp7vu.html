    Q:

    ELI5: Why can we catch a slight buzz shortly after a sip of alcohol even though it hasn’t been digested yet?

    cotyak A:

    Most of the alcohol is absorbed through the small intestine. However, about 20% of each sip of alcohol is absorbed through the mouth, throat, and stomach. Also, alcohol is metabolized by the body, not digested.

    Source: I teach safe alcohol service to servers and bartenders for state certification.
