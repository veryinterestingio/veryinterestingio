    Q:

    ELI5: Why does bacon grease seem to never go bad even without refrigeration, but other grease (e.g., hamburger grease) will go rancid in the same conditions?

    100PercentSethanol A:

    It definitely will go bad. It just may take longer because bacon is a salted cured meat without a lot of moisture, so the grease has a ton of salt and not much residual water, which inhibits bacteria growth.
