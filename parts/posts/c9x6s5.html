    ELI5: What happens to the brain when you zone out?

    ign_lifesaver2 A:

    The brain decouples attention from outward sensation. It decides that nothing too important, difficult or dangerous is happening out there, and cuts the connection between the external and internal worlds.

    Your brain works at memory retrieval of things important to your own life, your future, your relationships etc - things you know a lot about.
