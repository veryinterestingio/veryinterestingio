    Q:

    How does saline nasal spray moisturize your nose? I thought salt was used to dry things out?

    alteranmage A:

    It matches the salt concentration present in your cells (isotonic). Pure water would flow into the cells of your nasal passage and some would burst (lyse). Oversalinated solution would draw out water just like you assume it would. Balanced salinity is best.

    Edit: spelling, draw out, not dry out
