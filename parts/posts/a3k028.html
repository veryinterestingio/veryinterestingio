    Q:

    ELI5: What is that sinking feeling you experience when you get terrible news?

    frisdisc A:

    It's your body's fight or flight response. You pump blood away from organs used for eating/digesting/other bodily processes, and towards muscles and extremities used to run or fight. This gives your stomach that classic sinking feeling.
