    Q:

    What happens when you project an electron(like in projectile motion)? Does it behave as a particle or wave?

    RobusEtCeleritas A:

    It behaves as both. Its wavelength is inversely proportional to its momentum, and its wavelength gives the order of magnitude of the length scale at which its wavelike behavior becomes important.
