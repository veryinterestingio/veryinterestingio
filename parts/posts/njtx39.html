    Q:

    What is the difference between Photofission and Photodisintegration?

    RobusEtCeleritas A:

    Photofission is fission induced by a photon, and photodisintegration is just any time a photon knocks a particle (like a proton, neutron, alpha particle) out of a nucleus.
