Lithuim A:

Often there are still some predators, poisons that dissuade birds won’t necessarily also work on tarantulas.

A species with no predators at all can hit the “carrying capacity” of the environment - there’s only so much easy food to go around and a population larger than that will starve.

Tiny prey animals aren’t likely to hit that, but large grazers and apex predators often do.
