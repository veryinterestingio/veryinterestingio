mb34i A:

This is the typical sewer pipe setup.

The water in your toilet bowl and in the U pipe under your sink prevent the sewage smell gases from getting into your rooms. However, these gases must be released or pressure will build up, so there's a pipe vent going to the roof. So when the wind blows strongly outside, there's a bit of a chimney effect and some of that atmospheric wind "pressure" pushes on the water in your toilet bowl a little bit, enough to create waves.
