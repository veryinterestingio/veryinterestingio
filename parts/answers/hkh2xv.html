I am researching and writing about the COVID-19 clinical trials. Part of that has been proposing we standardize how we run antiviral clinical trials so we can easily compare their results and fill in report cards that cover all of the key outcomes. But I just see a much messier situation with human trials where the follow-up times are relatively random and key outcomes often get completely ignored.

Since most vaccine trial results do not get posted on www.clinicaltrials.gov I expect many of the current COVID-19 trial results will similarly go unposted.
