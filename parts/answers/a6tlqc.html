AVeryPrettyMan A:

I’m a pediatric occupational therapist so a lot of my day is spent working on handwriting for school aged kids. So the short answer is our handwriting is influenced by a number of things; hand strength, finger dexterity/fine motor control (how well you move your fingers in small movements), our vision, and our core strength (mainly our posture aka how we sit while writing). So since everyone doesn’t have the same strength or skill in these categories it’ll vary from person to person.
