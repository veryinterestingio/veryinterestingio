Some argue that predators actually do not target any prey in particular and they simply tend to catch the weakened ones, but most professionals agree on the former.

Obviously some animals are more intelligent than others, but I would have to say yes for a lot of "smarter" animals! Dogs certainly pick up on human emotion so I see no reason why they couldn't recognize an injury if its reasonably obvious
