That “urge” that you’re referring to, which I assume you may experience when first waking up. It’s called “pandiculation” which is yawning and stretching at the same time. Your muscles stretch and contract during pandiculation, it feels boom. It’s an involuntary response which somewhat resets your central nervous system from sleeping/resting to being awake (off to on) and can be observed in other animal species.

Source: https://pubmed.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/21665102
