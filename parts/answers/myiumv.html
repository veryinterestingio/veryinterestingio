AberrantRambler A:

You set an alarm to wake you up. There are two possibilities - it wakes you up or you wake up before it.

If it wakes you up you don’t remember it because it just did what it was supposed to - why would you bother remembering something that’s supposed to happen.

If you wake up significantly before the alarm you just go back to sleep.

If it’s only a little bit before the alarm you’re not falling back asleep before the alarm or are and experiencing time smear from light sleeping.

The alarm is eliminating the cases where you’d wake up just after the alarm would have gone off.
