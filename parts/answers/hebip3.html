MooncalfSirloin A:

It's caused by the way american elections work. The country is divided into little areas and each area gets a certain number of points. To win the presidency you need the most points. Each area votes independently from all the others and all the points go to the one candidate with the most votes.

This makes people afraid that if they don't make someone else win, that the candidate that they don't want is going to win. So people gather behind the candidates that is most likely to win - instead of the candidate that they feel best represents themselves.
