Depends on the plant. Some plants like navel oranges were found as a mutant plant, or part of a plant. People cut off branches and grafted them to the roots of other orange trees to make more seedless orange trees.

Some plants will grow fruit without seeds if the flower does not get fertilized. Growers cover the flowers, or clip off the female part, or do something else to keep them from getting fertilized.

Some plants will grow seedless fruit if they are a hybrid.

Some plants grow seedless fruit when the seeds themselves are fertilized by another species, because the plant embryo dies before it can grow into a seed.
