Your body goes into a fight-or-flight state, which is to say, the sympathetic nervous system gets activated. Mediated by adrenaline, it diverts blood away from less immediately important organs, such as the gastro-intestinal system, towards more acutely necessary organs, such as the brain and muscles.

This diverting away of blood from the stomach gives the sinking feeling.
