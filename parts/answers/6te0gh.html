Nakiranda A:

There is a differens between being able too smell something when you are trying really hard and noticing that there is a smell in the room that you just entered.

Getting pregnant won't affect the first of these two but it can/will affect the later.

There is something called the odor detection threshold, it's the the lowest concentration of a certain odor compound that is perceivable by the human sense of smell. That's you trying really hard too detect if there is a smell.

I don't have the english name for the other threshold. This threshold describes at which level you can notice a smell without being asked about it. It's this threshold that gets lower in pregnant women.

Short version: Pregnancy doesn't make you able too smell better just makes you notice smells more.
