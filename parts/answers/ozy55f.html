We didn't evolve in forests full of trees to climb. We evolved in grasslands to walk across. As such we are the most dominant species on the planet in persistence hunting. There is now one species of sled dog that is superior to us in endurance, but we specifically breed it to be that way.

Two legs saves a ton of energy to move. A mix of slow and fast twitch muscle fibers makes us able to hike all day and yet still have enough explosive speed to kill effectively. We were the zombie horde of our evolutionary era: relatively slow and weak, but never stopping. In fact, there are still tribes in Africa today that hunt using the exact same method
