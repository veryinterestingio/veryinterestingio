Interestingly enough, accelerating masses actually create their own event horizon, called a Rindler horizon, due to there existing regions of space time that are causally disconnected from the accelerating mass. That is, at some distance, a light speed particle could not catch up to a constantly accelerating mass.

This actually produces some interesting effects as the speed increases. Similar to how black holes should produce Hawking radiation, Rindler horizons should produce Unruh radiation, and accelerating a mass to sufficient speeds will effectively torch your rocket ship
