agnurse A:

One of the things about colds is that they aren't all caused by the same virus, or even the same family of viruses. Adenoviruses, coronaviruses, and rhinoviruses, among others, can ALL cause colds. The viruses can absolutely differ from one region of the world to another, and this could well be why you're getting sick more frequently.
