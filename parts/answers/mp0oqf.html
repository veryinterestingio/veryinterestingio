This has less to do with evolution and more to do with the reward prediction system in the brain. We get hungry at the sight of a meal because we are always rewarded with food shortly after seeing a prepared meal.

If we went back to a hunt + gather system today, it wouldn't take long for our brains to associate the reward with our living prey.
