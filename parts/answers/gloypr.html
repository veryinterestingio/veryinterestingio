In my swimming lessons way-back-when (the 1970's ) I witnessed a demonstration of this.

There was one person laying on their back on the bottom of the pool in the shallow end ( chest deep). They were holding a weight to stay down. There were two other people standing beside one on the left and one on the right.

The two standing people were taking a breath and then alternately doing mouth-to-mouth with the person in the water to transfer the air.

So this was transferring air twice as fast as one person could do it.

But this demonstration went on for some time, like several minutes. It was obvious that they could have done it indefinitely.
