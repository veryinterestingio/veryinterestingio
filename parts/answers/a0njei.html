Another factor that I haven't seen commented yet, is the fact that people are able to successfully bring larger babies to term. Thanks to medical advances (c-sections and the like), mothers and their babies are less likely to die during childbirth. Notably when a much larger baby (9+ lbs) is involved. It used to be uncommon for both the mother and child to survive if the baby was 14 lbs, but now, that's a relatively common occurrence (as far as 14 lb babies are concerned).
