exploringspace_ A:

Economies of scale! On a large enough property you can grow tens of thousands of these simultaneously. Although the first plant needs a long time to grow and produce its first fruit (a quick google search said ~2 years) the subsequent fruits take just over a year, since the plant is already there. Also, your expenses are much lower with farming than with mass production of industrial or technological products because sunlight, rain and soil (to an extent) are free, and require very few workers
