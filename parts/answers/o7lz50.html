Is the Van Der Waals force still believed to be behind most reptile wall adhesion? There is a 2014 paper that concludes the following for geckos:

"We have demonstrated that it is the CE-driven electrostatic interactions which dictate the strength of gecko adhesion, and not the Van der Waals or capillary forces which are conventionally considered as the main source of gecko adhesion."

https://www.ncbi.nlm.nih.gov/pmc/articles/PMC4233685/

The wiki article for Van Der Waals force also references this finding.
