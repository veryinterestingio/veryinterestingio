Here’s a reasonable discussion of it (if you can ignore the ads and opening paragraphs)

1) Loss of smell is fairly common in upper-respiratory viral infections. It’s temporary

2) Most of taste is smell, so that’s likely why taste is affected

3) Seems like the mechanism for asnomia is not super-well understood, but it seems like the cilia on your smelling cells go away, and/or the virus causes inflammation of the olfactory nerve causing it to not work well, and/or your nasal cavities are just blocked up by mucous or swelling
