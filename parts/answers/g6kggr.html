In a prefect flat space-time? Yes.

Newtons law: an object in motion will remain in motion until a force acts upon it.

However any minor object, even as small as photons or hydrogen atoms will affect your speed.

So someone shines a Lazer at you, your speed changes, you get hit by the stream of particles coming from the sun, your speed changes.

You pass a large body and get affected by the gravity, your speed changes.
