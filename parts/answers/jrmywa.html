Arkalius A:

There isn't as distinct a difference between left and right for us as there is between up and down, and forward and back. Our bodies are symmetrical left to right and there are no objective differences between the two directions for us generally. Up is above our head and away from gravity and down is toward the rest of our body and gravity. Forward is the direction our body is facing and back is opposite that. Left and right are essentially the same so we have to do a little more mental processing to distinguish those directions with the words we use for them.
