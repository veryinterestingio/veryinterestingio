FalseDmitriy A:

The family is called the Sygnathids, which includes seahorses and seadragons and pipefish. If you look up pipefish, you'll see that they look like a stretched-out, less horsey version of a seahorse: similar eyes, jaws, and mouth, but long and slender like, well, a pipe. Seadragons are shaped like a cross between the two, with a more seahorse-shaped body but without the seahorse's characteristic upright body position while swimming. All the Sygnathids do the thing where the male incubates the eggs, either in a pouch or attached to his tail.
