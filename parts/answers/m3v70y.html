There is some interesting research on this topic; that the skin's microbiome (the bacteria that live on our skin, and feed on our secretions, etc) actually has a protective role to play against uv light. I would assume that taking antibiotics has a deleterious affect on these bacteria this causing the photosensitivity.

It's a fascinating, and growing line of research.
