Adept_Historian_7175 A:

I bought a retro style table fan a few days ago. As soon as I turned it on, I was back at my grandparent’s house, 38 years ago, listening to the oscillating fan in their living room. Memory is a crazy thing. Something as simple as the sound of fan blades triggered a place and time I hadn’t really thought about in years. I didn’t even realize that particular sound had that association till it happened. Now - it brings me unexpected joy every time I use it and the associated memories flood back - canning vegetables, picking up pecans from under the tree, putting pennies on the railroad tracks (it was a simpler time), reading under the shade tree. I love the sound of that stupid fan now, and all I bought it for was some extra air flow in my office.
