smolbur A:

To bake something means to thoroughly cook something throughout evenly in an oven.

To roast something means you expose something to an intense heat so it crisps up on the outside.

Roasting is done in a shorter time at a higher temperature to give variation in texture.

Baking is done over a longer time at a lower temperature to achieve an even texture.
