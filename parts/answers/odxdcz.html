Crossing fingers was commonly used as a sort of protective charm for warding off evil. This explains why people wishing you luck would cross their fingers, as well as why those breaking a promise would do it. Someone breaking an oath might often be swearing that oath by someone or something, with the implication that if they broke it some unspecified spiritual or karmic consequences would follow. By crossing their fingers they were symbolically attempting to ward off those consequences.
