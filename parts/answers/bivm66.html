fhost344 A:

I think they look for certain cues, like gravity (how far up from ground to build) and darkness (means a protected place possibly, and also possibly a wetter and cooler place = flies). The cues that they look for are likely species dependent. Also, they just kind of get lucky,: spider moms have hundreds of babies... The lucky ones that pick good spots are the ones that survive.
