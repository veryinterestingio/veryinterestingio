Most people are reflexively resistant to change, even when that change could or will have a positive effect on them.

Change involves the possibility of negative risk, causing you to lose time or resources. When people evaluate their situation, they often feel safer avoiding negative risk entirely than pursuing a positive risk/opportunity.
