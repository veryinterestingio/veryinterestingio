https://www.thoughtco.com/how-do-dolphins-sleep-2291489

I cannot find anything on "personality", but when dolphins sleep, they do not do much "personable" stuff. They stay still or swim slowly and in a straight line. They do not hunt, they do not play, they do not interact with other dolphins.

We do not know how dolphins feel (since they cannot tell us), but it might be similar to how you feel when watching Netflix at 2am -- you are kinda aware of what is going on around you, but you do not feel like moving or responding to anything that is not life-threatening.
