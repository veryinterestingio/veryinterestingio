musicalfurball A:

There appears to be a considerable difference in the prefrontal cortex of autism spectrum individuals which impacts executive functioning. There also appears to be variations in how the various brain networks interact, specifically impacting something called the default mode network or DMN. These two changes seem to account for the behavioral, sensory, and processing differences observed in these people. Since these changes are structural, it's almost definitely primarily a genetic change, although there may also be hormonal interactions in utero. This could explain why it's a spectrum rather than an on/off.

100% it is definitely not caused by any post-partem environmental factor, including vaccines.
