tealeaf64 A:

Smoking a lot of cannabis during adolescence can increase your risk of developing psychosis. That's fairly well substantiated. We don't 100% know why, but it's thought to be related to something called neural pruning that takes place in puberty continuing into your mid twenties, in which little used synapses are elimitated. Heavy cannabis use seems to interfere with this process.
