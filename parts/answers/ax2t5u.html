ZiahSmith A:

We know sunscreen blocks UV rays thereby reducing the risk of skin cancer, but we don’t know if the chemicals themselves are healthy to put on our skin over a long period of time. There is a chance they get absorbed and are carcinogenic themselves. Not to mention sunscreen is uncomfortable, oily and smelly.

I think a better option is to find clothing that covers and protects from UV. Look for clothes with listed UPF over 30. I’m a big fan of “sun hoodies” and “fishing hoodies” for hiking, and even wear sun gloves because I burn easily.
