Your heart is a pump, spreading blood throughout your body. Every time it pumps blood flows through your veins and arteries. Your body is designed to ignore the blood flow as it's just a normal process that there is no need to distract you with. You very rarely think about your own breathing, but now that i mentioned your breathing you are noticing every breath you take right now. Things like injuries can make you aware of that process.

Smashed my finger so bad few years back the nail fell off, for 2 days it throbbed every time my heart pumped blood to it because of the swelling.
