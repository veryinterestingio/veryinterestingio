But, there are powerful lobbies that don't want that to happen. The largest of them are the tax preparation industry who makes a lot of money that they would stand to lose if the process was easier.

It's literally because tax prep companies want to charge you money every single year for their services or software. Most developed countries use a pre-filled tax form that works exactly like you say. The government has all your info, so they compute the best return for you, they send you the form, you sign it (or make corrections, or redo the entire thing yourself if you really want to for some reason), and send it back. The US is very unique in that we don't do something similar to this.
